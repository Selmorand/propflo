# PropFlo WhatsApp Scripts

## Purpose

This document contains draft WhatsApp scripts for PropFlo seller leads and partner enquiries.

The goal is to keep WhatsApp communication clear, short, professional, and controlled.

All scripts must be reviewed and approved by PropFlo before use.

## 1. Main Principle

WhatsApp should be used for short, practical communication.

It should not be used for unapproved financial promises, detailed repayment discussions, or sensitive document handling unless PropFlo has approved the process.

## 2. New Seller Lead Message

### Timing

After a seller submits an enquiry.

### Script

Good day [First Name], this is [Name] from PropFlo.

Thank you for your enquiry. We have received your details and would like to confirm a few points about your property sale so we can see whether PropFlo may be able to assist.

When would be a convenient time to call you?

## 3. Missed Call Follow-Up

### Timing

After calling the lead without success.

### Script

Good day [First Name], this is [Name] from PropFlo.

We tried to reach you regarding your enquiry. Please let us know when it would be convenient to call you back.

## 4. Basic Qualification Follow-Up

### Timing

After the lead replies and is ready to continue.

### Script

Thank you.

To check the next step, we may need to confirm a few basic details:

1. Has the property been sold?
2. Has an Offer to Purchase been signed?
3. Has a transferring attorney been appointed?
4. Where is the property located?
5. What amount are you hoping to access?

Assistance is subject to qualification, verification, approval, and PropFlo's terms and conditions.

Final wording must be approved by PropFlo.

## 5. Document Checklist Message

### Timing

After PropFlo confirms the lead may continue.

### Script

Good day [First Name].

To continue with your PropFlo enquiry, please prepare the documents requested by your consultant.

These may include:

- Signed Offer to Purchase
- Seller identity document
- Property details
- Transferring attorney details
- Bond or settlement information, if applicable

Please only send documents through the approved PropFlo process.

## 6. Document Reminder Message

### Timing

One working day after documents were requested, if not received.

### Script

Good day [First Name].

This is a reminder that we are waiting for the requested documents for your PropFlo enquiry.

Once received, the team can continue reviewing your application.

## 7. Not Qualified Message

### Timing

After PropFlo confirms the enquiry does not qualify.

### Script

Good day [First Name].

Based on the information provided, it does not look like your enquiry fits the current PropFlo requirements.

Thank you for contacting us.

Final wording must be approved by PropFlo.

## 8. Final Follow-Up Message

### Timing

After agreed follow-up attempts fail.

### Script

Good day [First Name].

We have not been able to continue with your PropFlo enquiry.

If you still need assistance, please reply to this message. Otherwise, your enquiry may be marked as inactive.

## 9. Opt-Out Message

### Timing

When a person asks not to be contacted again.

### Script

No problem. We have noted your request and will not contact you further regarding this enquiry.

## 10. Estate Agent Partner Introduction

### Timing

When contacting an estate agent or replying to partner interest.

### Script

Good day [First Name], this is [Name] from PropFlo.

PropFlo helps qualifying property sellers explore bridging finance linked to their property sale before transfer is complete.

If you work with sellers who may need this type of assistance, we can send you the approved partner information and referral process.

Would you like us to send the details?

## 11. Conveyancer Information Message

### Timing

When contacting a conveyancer or replying to a professional enquiry.

### Script

Good day [First Name], this is [Name] from PropFlo.

PropFlo provides information for property transfer professionals who want to understand how seller bridging finance may fit into the transfer process.

We can send approved information about the process, documentation considerations, and communication boundaries.

Would you like us to send the information pack?

## 12. Partner Follow-Up Message

### Timing

After partner information was sent.

### Script

Good day [First Name].

I am following up to check whether you received the PropFlo partner information.

Please let us know if you have any questions or would like to discuss the referral process.

## 13. What Not To Send On WhatsApp

Avoid:

- You are approved.
- You are guaranteed to qualify.
- Funds will be paid today.
- No documents are needed.
- There are no costs.
- This is risk-free.
- We can definitely help.
- Do not worry about the terms.
- Send your sensitive documents here.

## 14. WhatsApp Recording Rules

Every WhatsApp interaction should be recorded in the CRM or lead tracker.

Record:

- Date
- Message sent
- Lead response
- Lead stage
- Next follow-up date
- Notes

## 15. Approval Notes

Before using these scripts, PropFlo must approve:

- Seller wording
- Partner wording
- Qualification wording
- Document wording
- Disclaimer wording
- Opt-out wording
- Privacy wording

## 16. Starting Recommendation

Start with these three scripts only:

1. New seller lead message
2. Missed call follow-up
3. Document checklist message

Add more scripts after the sales process is tested.
