# PropFlo Email Sequences

## Purpose

This document contains draft email sequences for PropFlo seller leads and partner enquiries.

The goal is to support the sales process with clear, useful, approved follow-up communication.

All email wording must be reviewed and approved by PropFlo before use.

## 1. Main Principle

Email should support human follow-up, not replace it.

For bridging finance enquiries, people may need quick contact from a consultant. Email should confirm the enquiry, explain next steps, and reduce confusion.

## 2. Seller Lead Email Sequence

### Email 1: Enquiry Received

### Timing

Immediately after form submission.

### Subject

Your PropFlo enquiry has been received

### Body

Good day [First Name],

Thank you for your enquiry.

Your information has been received and a PropFlo consultant will review your details.

If your enquiry was submitted during working hours, someone will contact you as soon as possible. If it was submitted after hours, you should be contacted on the next working day.

Please note that any assistance is subject to qualification, verification, approval, and PropFlo's terms and conditions.

Kind regards,  
The PropFlo Team

## 3. Seller Email 2: Next Step Confirmation

### Timing

After initial contact or qualification check.

### Subject

Next steps for your PropFlo enquiry

### Body

Good day [First Name],

Thank you for speaking with PropFlo.

The next step is to confirm the information needed to review your enquiry.

A consultant may ask for details about your property sale, Offer to Purchase, transferring attorney, and the amount required.

Please only send documents through the approved PropFlo process.

Kind regards,  
The PropFlo Team

## 4. Seller Email 3: Document Checklist

### Timing

When documents are requested.

### Subject

Documents needed for your PropFlo enquiry

### Body

Good day [First Name],

To continue with your PropFlo enquiry, please prepare the documents requested by your consultant.

These may include:

- Signed Offer to Purchase
- Seller identity document
- Property details
- Transferring attorney details
- Bond or settlement information, if applicable
- Any additional documents requested by PropFlo

Please only send documents through the approved PropFlo process.

Kind regards,  
The PropFlo Team

## 5. Seller Email 4: Document Reminder

### Timing

One working day after documents are requested, if not received.

### Subject

Reminder: PropFlo documents requested

### Body

Good day [First Name],

This is a reminder that we are waiting for the documents requested for your PropFlo enquiry.

Once the documents are received, the PropFlo team can continue reviewing your application.

If you no longer need assistance, please let us know.

Kind regards,  
The PropFlo Team

## 6. Seller Email 5: Final Follow-Up

### Timing

Five to seven working days after no response.

### Subject

Should we keep your PropFlo enquiry open?

### Body

Good day [First Name],

We have not been able to continue with your PropFlo enquiry.

If you still need assistance, please reply to this email or contact PropFlo directly.

If we do not hear from you, your enquiry may be marked as inactive.

Kind regards,  
The PropFlo Team

## 7. Not Qualified Email

### Timing

After PropFlo confirms the lead does not qualify.

### Subject

Your PropFlo enquiry

### Body

Good day [First Name],

Thank you for contacting PropFlo.

Based on the information provided, it does not look like your enquiry fits the current PropFlo requirements.

Thank you for your time.

Kind regards,  
The PropFlo Team

Final wording must be approved by PropFlo.

## 8. Partner Email Sequence

## 9. Partner Email 1: Partner Enquiry Received

### Timing

Immediately after partner form submission.

### Subject

Thank you for your PropFlo partner enquiry

### Body

Good day [First Name],

Thank you for your interest in PropFlo.

PropFlo works with qualifying property sellers who may need access to funds linked to their property sale before transfer is complete.

A PropFlo representative will contact you to explain the referral process and approved wording.

Kind regards,  
The PropFlo Team

## 10. Partner Email 2: Referral Process

### Timing

After first partner contact.

### Subject

How the PropFlo referral process works

### Body

Good day [First Name],

The PropFlo referral process is designed to keep communication clear and professional.

The basic process is:

1. You identify a seller who may need assistance.
2. You use approved wording only.
3. The seller agrees to be introduced.
4. PropFlo contacts the seller directly.
5. PropFlo checks whether the seller may qualify.
6. PropFlo manages the enquiry process with the seller.

Partners should not promise approval, payout timing, costs, or outcomes.

Kind regards,  
The PropFlo Team

## 11. Partner Email 3: Approved Wording

### Timing

After partner expresses interest.

### Subject

Approved wording for PropFlo introductions

### Body

Good day [First Name],

When introducing PropFlo to a seller, please use careful and approved wording.

Suggested wording:

PropFlo may be able to assist qualifying sellers who need access to funds linked to their property sale before transfer is complete.

If you would like, I can introduce you to PropFlo so their team can explain the process and check whether you may qualify.

Please avoid promising approval, payout timing, fees, or outcomes.

Kind regards,  
The PropFlo Team

Final wording must be approved by PropFlo.

## 12. Email Compliance Notes

Before use, PropFlo must approve:

- Product wording
- Qualification wording
- Timing wording
- Fee wording
- Repayment wording
- Disclaimer wording
- Privacy wording
- Document request wording
- Partner wording

## 13. Starting Recommendation

Start with only the essential emails:

1. Seller enquiry received
2. Seller document checklist
3. Partner enquiry received

Add the rest after the real sales process has been tested.
