# PropFlo Content Calendar

## Purpose

This document defines the starting content calendar for PropFlo.

The goal is to plan useful content for the first 90 days without creating random posts or weak articles.

## 1. Main Principle

Content should support the marketing funnel.

Every piece of content should help with at least one of these goals:

- Explain the offer
- Build trust
- Answer seller questions
- Support Google Ads landing pages
- Improve SEO
- Support partner referrals
- Reduce repeated sales questions
- Improve conversion

## 2. First 90-Day Content Focus

### Month 1

Focus on foundation content.

Priority content:

- Seller landing page
- FAQ page
- How it works page
- Check if you qualify form wording
- Approved disclaimers
- Basic partner wording

### Month 2

Focus on educational content.

Priority content:

- What is property bridging finance?
- Can I access money before property transfer?
- What documents are needed?
- LinkedIn partner posts
- Basic social education posts

### Month 3

Focus on trust and partner content.

Priority content:

- How long does property transfer take?
- What happens if transfer is delayed?
- Estate agent partner content
- Conveyancer information content
- Partner FAQ content

## 3. Monthly Publishing Recommendation

Recommended minimum publishing rhythm:

- 2 blog or educational articles per month
- 2 LinkedIn posts per week
- 2 Facebook posts per week
- 2 Instagram posts per week, if used
- 1 partner outreach email or message set per month
- FAQ updates based on real lead questions

## 7. Content Approval Statuses

Use these statuses:

- Not started
- Drafting
- Internal review
- Sent to PropFlo
- Changes requested
- Approved
- Published
- Paused

## 8. Content Rules

Every public content item must be checked for:

- Approved wording
- Correct disclaimer
- No unapproved timing claims
- No unapproved fee claims
- No guaranteed approval wording
- No personal loan-style messaging
- Clear CTA
- Correct landing page link
- UTM tracking where needed
