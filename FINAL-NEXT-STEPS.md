This document explains what to do next now that the PropFlo marketing project structure has been created.

## 4. What You Need From PropFlo

Before finalising the proposal, PropFlo must confirm:

- Legal entity details
- Whether PropFlo is the lender, broker, introducer, platform, or brand
- Registration or compliance details, if applicable
- Product structure
- Who qualifies
- Who does not qualify
- Fees
- Repayment process
- Approval process
- Payout process
- Required disclaimers
- Privacy policy
- Terms and conditions
- Lead handling process
- Budget comfort level
- Access and assets

## 5. Meeting Goal

The next meeting should answer this:

Can PropFlo support a structured 90-day marketing launch with the correct product clarity, compliance sign-off, budget, lead handling, and reporting process?

## 6. Do Not Launch Ads Yet

Do not launch Google Ads, Meta Ads, LinkedIn Ads, or partner outreach until:

- Compliance questions are answered
- Claims are approved
- Landing pages are approved
- Tracking is tested
- Lead handover is confirmed
- Budget is approved
- Client sign-off is recorded

## 10. Final Rule

The first 90 days must be treated as a controlled launch.

The aim is not to spend heavily.

The aim is to prove:

- Which channels generate qualified leads
- Which leads become applications
- Which applications become funded deals
- What the real cost per funded deal looks like
- Whether the system can scale responsibly
