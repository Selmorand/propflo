# PropFlo Design Prompts

## Purpose

This document contains draft creative prompts for PropFlo advertising, social media graphics, landing page visuals, and partner material.

The goal is to create consistent, professional visuals without making PropFlo look like a payday loan or emergency cash brand.

## 1. Main Visual Principle

PropFlo visuals should feel:

- Professional
- Calm
- Trustworthy
- Property-focused
- Finance-aware
- Simple
- Clear
- Responsible

Avoid visuals that feel like:

- Payday loans
- Emergency cash
- Debt rescue
- Desperate finance
- Luxury lifestyle promises
- High-pressure sales

## 2. General Style Prompt

Create a clean professional marketing graphic for PropFlo, a property seller bridging finance brand. The design should feel trustworthy, calm, modern, and property-focused. Use simple geometric layout, clear typography, a subtle property transfer timeline, and restrained finance symbolism. Avoid cash piles, desperate people, urgent red warning styles, luxury lifestyle imagery, or exaggerated loan advertising.

## 3. Concept: The Transfer Gap

### Prompt

Create a clean professional graphic showing the gap between a property sale being accepted and transfer being completed. Use a simple horizontal timeline with these stages: Offer Accepted, Transfer Process, Proceeds Paid. Show PropFlo as a calm bridge across the waiting period. The design should be minimal, trustworthy, and suitable for a finance-related property brand. Avoid cash imagery and aggressive sales styling.

### Suggested Text

Property sold, but still waiting for transfer?

### Use For

- Seller landing page
- Meta retargeting
- Facebook post
- Instagram post
- Partner explainer

## 4. Concept: Property Sale Timeline

### Prompt

Create a simple professional infographic showing a property seller journey from signed Offer to Purchase to property transfer completion. Use clean icons for document, house, attorney, verification, and payout. The design must be easy to understand, calm, and businesslike. Use minimal text and strong visual order.

### Suggested Text

There is often a gap between sale and payout.

### Use For

- How it works page
- FAQ page
- Blog article
- Partner PDF

## 5. Concept: Check If You Qualify

### Prompt

Create a professional digital advert for PropFlo focused on checking eligibility for property seller bridging finance. Show a clean checklist next to a simple house icon and document icon. The design should communicate clarity, trust, and responsible process. Avoid promises of approval, cash, speed, or guaranteed outcomes.

### Suggested Text

Check if you may qualify

### Use For

- Google Ads landing page graphic
- Meta retargeting
- Seller CTA section

## 6. Concept: Document Checklist

### Prompt

Include simple icons for signed offer, ID document, property details, transferring attorney, and bond information. The design should be clear, helpful, and professional, with no dramatic finance imagery.

### Suggested Text

- FAQ page
- Social post
- Landing page section

## 13. Visual Rules

Use:

- Clear typography
- Simple icons
- Property symbols
- Timeline diagrams
- Checklist layouts
- Process flows
- Calm colour palette
- Professional spacing

Avoid:

- Cash piles
- Handfuls of money
- Red emergency designs
- Desperate people
- Luxury cars
- Mansions
- Countdown timers
- Guaranteed approval badges
- Payday loan visual style

## 14. Approval Notes

Before use, PropFlo must approve:

- Graphic headline
- Supporting text
- CTA wording
- Disclaimer wording
- Any claim about speed, approval, fees, or repayment
- Partner wording
- Use of logo and brand colours
