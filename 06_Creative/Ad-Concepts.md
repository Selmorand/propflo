# PropFlo Ad Concepts

## Purpose

This document defines initial advertising concepts for PropFlo.

The goal is to create clear campaign ideas that can be used for Google Ads, Meta retargeting, LinkedIn posts, landing page sections, and creative briefs.

All concepts must be reviewed and approved by PropFlo before publication.

## 1. Main Creative Principle

PropFlo ads should not look like emergency loan adverts.

The creative should be professional, property-focused, and trust-based.

The message should focus on the timing gap between selling a property and receiving the proceeds after transfer.

## 2. Core Campaign Idea

### Concept Name

The Transfer Gap

### Main Idea

A seller has sold a property but still has to wait for the transfer process to complete before receiving funds.

PropFlo helps qualifying sellers explore funding linked to their property sale before transfer is complete.

### Possible Headline

Property sold, but still waiting for transfer?

### Possible CTA

Check if you qualify

### Best Use

- Google Ads landing page
- Meta retargeting
- Facebook explainer post
- Instagram carousel
- Seller brochure

## 3. Concept: The Waiting Period

### Main Idea

Property sellers often think the sale is complete once the offer is accepted, but the money only comes later.

### Possible Headline

The offer is signed. The money is not paid yet.

### Supporting Copy

The transfer process can take time. PropFlo helps qualifying sellers understand whether bridging finance may be an option while waiting for transfer.

### Best Use

- Blog visual
- Social post
- Landing page section
- Retargeting ad

## 4. Concept: The Property Sale Timeline

### Main Idea

Show a simple timeline from offer accepted to transfer complete, with a funding gap in the middle.

### Suggested Visual

A clean horizontal timeline:

Offer accepted ? Documents ? Transfer process ? Proceeds paid

PropFlo sits between:

Transfer process ? Proceeds paid

### Possible Headline

There is often a gap between sale and payout.

### Best Use

- Landing page graphic
- Partner PDF
- Estate agent explainer
- LinkedIn post

## 5. Concept: Check If You Qualify

### Main Idea

The safest CTA is not aggressive. It invites the seller to check whether they may qualify.

### Possible Headline

Not sure if bridging finance applies to your sale?

### Supporting Copy

Start with a simple enquiry. A PropFlo consultant can explain the next step.

### Best Use

- Google Ads
- Landing page CTA
- Retargeting ad
- Thank-you page

## 6. Concept: For Estate Agents

### Main Idea

Agents often see seller pressure before anyone else.

### Possible Headline

When your seller is waiting for transfer, timing matters.

### Supporting Copy

PropFlo helps qualifying sellers explore funding linked to their property sale before transfer is complete.

### Best Use

- LinkedIn post
- Partner PDF
- Estate agent landing page
- Direct outreach email

## 7. Concept: For Conveyancers

### Main Idea

A professional, restrained campaign aimed at conveyancing firms.

### Possible Headline

Clear information for property transfer professionals.

### Supporting Copy

PropFlo provides information for professionals who want to understand how seller bridging finance may fit into the transfer process.

### Best Use

- LinkedIn
- Email outreach
- Conveyancer information page
- Partner PDF

## 8. Concept: Document Checklist

### Main Idea

Reduce friction by showing sellers what they may need before applying.

### Possible Headline

What should you prepare before enquiring?

### Suggested Visual

A simple checklist:

- Signed Offer to Purchase
- Seller identity document
- Property details
- Transferring attorney details
- Bond or settlement details, if applicable

### Best Use

- FAQ page
- Email follow-up
- Social post
- WhatsApp follow-up

## 9. Concept: No Promises, Clear Process

### Main Idea

Use trust and transparency as a differentiator.

### Possible Headline

No hype. Just a clear qualification process.

### Supporting Copy

PropFlo enquiries are subject to qualification, verification, approval, and terms and conditions.

### Best Use

- Trust section
- LinkedIn post
- Partner material
- FAQ content

## 11. Visual Style

Use:

- Clean layout
- Simple icons
- Property transfer timeline
- Calm colours
- Professional typography
- Short copy
- Clear CTA

Avoid:

- Cash piles
- Desperate people
- Fake urgency
- Guaranteed approval language
- Red-alert emergency design
- Payday loan styling

## 12. Compliance Notes

Before use, PropFlo must approve:

- Headlines
- Supporting copy
- CTA wording
- Disclaimers
- Any timing claims
- Any approval wording
- Any fee wording
- Any partner wording

## 13. Starting Recommendation

Start with static graphics and landing page visuals.

Do not create expensive video ads until the main offer, landing page, and Google Search campaign are tested.
