# PropFlo Brand Guidelines

## Purpose

This document defines the starting brand and creative direction for PropFlo marketing.

The goal is to make all marketing material feel professional, trustworthy, clear, and consistent.

## 1. Main Brand Principle

PropFlo should not look like a payday loan brand, aggressive finance advert, or desperate cash offer.

The brand should feel:

- Professional
- Calm
- Trustworthy
- Clear
- Responsible
- Property-focused
- Finance-aware
- Human but not emotional or dramatic

## 2. Brand Position

PropFlo helps qualifying property sellers explore bridging finance linked to a property sale before transfer is complete.

The brand should sit between:

- Property
- Finance
- Conveyancing
- Seller support
- Cash-flow timing

It should not feel like:

- Payday lending
- Debt rescue
- Emergency loans
- Luxury lifestyle finance
- High-pressure sales

## 3. Visual Direction

Recommended visual style:

- Clean layouts
- Strong spacing
- Clear hierarchy
- Professional typography
- Simple icons
- Property-related visual cues
- Process diagrams
- Calm colour palette
- Trust-building design
- Minimal clutter

## 4. Suggested Visual Themes

Useful visual themes:

- Property transfer timeline
- Sale agreement to transfer process
- Bridge between sale and transfer
- House icon plus timeline
- Document checklist
- Seller journey
- Agent referral flow
- Conveyancer process support
- Funding gap illustration
- Simple step-by-step process

## 5. Visuals To Avoid

Avoid:

- People holding cash
- Piles of money
- Desperate facial expressions
- Red emergency graphics
- Fake urgency countdowns
- Luxury cars
- Mansion lifestyle imagery
- Overly emotional seller stress imagery
- Loan shark-style visuals
- Over-promising financial imagery

## 6. Copy Tone

Copy should be:

- Plain
- Clear
- Helpful
- Measured
- Calm
- Professional
- Specific
- Easy to understand

Avoid:

- Hype
- Fear
- Pressure
- Shame
- Overpromising
- Legal-sounding jargon
- Unapproved finance claims

## 7. Message Style

Good message style:

Property sold but still waiting for transfer?

PropFlo helps qualifying sellers explore funding linked to their property sale before transfer is complete.

Poor message style:

Need cash now? Get instant money today with guaranteed approval.

## 8. CTA Style

Recommended calls to action:

- Check if you qualify
- Speak to PropFlo
- Request a callback
- Learn how it works
- Start an enquiry
- Request partner information

Avoid:

- Get cash now
- Claim your money
- Apply instantly
- Guaranteed approval
- Urgent funding today
- No questions asked

## 9. Trust Signals

Where possible, marketing material should include:

- Clear process
- Clear qualification wording
- Clear disclaimers
- Privacy reassurance
- Contact details
- Legal entity information
- Terms and conditions link
- Privacy policy link
- Human support option
- Professional partner information

## 10. Landing Page Design Notes

Landing pages should:

- Explain the offer above the fold
- Make the next step obvious
- Use short sections
- Use clear headings
- Include FAQ content
- Explain qualification
- Explain that approval is not guaranteed
- Use trust sections
- Show contact options
- Keep forms simple at first

## 11. Social Graphic Notes

Social graphics should:

- Use one clear idea per graphic
- Avoid too much text
- Use strong headline hierarchy
- Support the message, not replace it
- Link back to the landing page or FAQ
- Use approved wording only

## 12. Partner Material Notes

Partner material should feel more formal than seller advertising.

Partner material should include:

- What PropFlo does
- Who may qualify
- When to refer
- What partners may say
- What partners must not promise
- Referral process
- Contact details

## 13. Compliance Notes

Before public use, PropFlo must approve:

- Logo use
- Legal entity wording
- Finance wording
- Disclaimers
- Partner wording
- Claims about approval
- Claims about payout timing
- Claims about fees or costs
- Claims about repayment

## 14. Starting Recommendation

Before creating final ads or landing pages, collect PropFlo's existing brand assets:

- Logo
- Colour codes
- Fonts
- Existing brochures
- Existing website screenshots
- Existing tone of voice
- Legal disclaimers
- Privacy policy
- Terms and conditions
