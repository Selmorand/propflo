# PropFlo Compliance Questions

## Purpose

This document captures the questions that must be answered before marketing, advertising, landing pages, email campaigns, WhatsApp follow-ups, or public claims are created.

Because PropFlo offers bridging finance to property sellers, all financial, legal, qualification, approval, fee, repayment, and timing claims must be confirmed by PropFlo before publication.

Interon can create and manage the marketing system, but PropFlo must approve all product, legal, financial, and compliance wording.

## 1. Business and Legal

1. What is the full legal name of the company offering the finance?
2. Is PropFlo the finance provider, broker, introducer, platform, or marketing brand?
3. Is the finance provider registered with the National Credit Regulator if required?
4. Are there licence numbers, registration numbers, or legal disclosures that must appear on the website?
5. Does the product fall under the National Credit Act, or is it exempt?
6. Who signs off all financial and legal wording before publication?
7. Are there any words or claims we may not use in advertising?

## 2. Product Terms

1. What type of bridging finance is offered?
2. Who qualifies?
3. Who does not qualify?
4. What is the minimum advance amount?
5. What is the maximum advance amount?
6. What percentage of the expected property sale proceeds can be advanced?
7. What fees apply?
8. Is interest charged?
9. Are admin fees charged?
10. Are legal or conveyancing fees charged?
11. When is repayment made?
12. Who makes repayment?
13. What happens if property transfer is delayed?
14. What happens if the property sale falls through?

## 3. Property Sale Requirements

1. Must the seller have an accepted Offer to Purchase?
2. Must the transferring attorney confirm the transaction?
3. Must bond cancellation figures be available?
4. Is a signed sale agreement required?
5. Is the property required to be bonded or unbonded?
6. Are sectional title, freehold, commercial, or agricultural properties treated differently?
7. Are there any provinces or areas not supported?

## 4. Application Process

1. What information must the seller provide?
2. What documents are required?
3. How does PropFlo verify the transaction?
4. How long does approval usually take?
5. How long does payout usually take after approval?
6. Who contacts the seller after the lead is submitted?
7. What is the preferred contact method: phone, email, WhatsApp, or form?

## 5. Approved Marketing Claims

Please confirm whether we may use the following phrases:

1. Bridging finance for property sellers.
2. Access funds before property transfer.
3. Property sold but waiting for transfer?
4. Get access to funds linked to your property sale.
5. Fast approval.
6. Same-day approval.
7. Funds within 24 hours.
8. No upfront cost.
9. No obligation quote.
10. Subject to approval.
11. Subject to qualification.
12. Terms and conditions apply.

## 6. Required Disclaimers

1. What disclaimer must appear on adverts?
2. What disclaimer must appear on landing pages?
3. What disclaimer must appear on forms?
4. What disclaimer must appear in emails?
5. What disclaimer must appear in WhatsApp messages?
6. Are representative examples required?
7. Must fees or repayment examples be shown?

## 7. POPIA and Data Protection

1. What personal information will be collected?
2. Where will leads be stored?
3. Who has access to the lead data?
4. How long will lead data be kept?
5. How will consent be captured?
6. How will unsubscribe requests be handled?
7. How will WhatsApp opt-outs be handled?
8. Does PropFlo already have a privacy policy?
9. Does PropFlo already have website terms and conditions?

## 8. Sales and Lead Handling

1. Who receives new leads?
2. How quickly will leads be contacted?
3. What is the target response time?
4. What makes a lead qualified?
5. What makes a lead unqualified?
6. What stages should be tracked in the CRM?
7. What information should be reported monthly?
8. What is the average funded deal value?
9. What is the average revenue or profit per funded deal?
10. What cost per funded deal would be acceptable?

## 9. Referral Partners

1. Will estate agents be referral partners?
2. Will conveyancers be referral partners?
3. Will bond originators be referral partners?
4. Will referral partners receive commission or referral fees?
5. What may partners say to clients?
6. What may partners not say to clients?
7. Is a partner agreement required?
