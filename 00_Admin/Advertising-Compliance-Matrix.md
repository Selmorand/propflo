# Advertising Compliance Matrix

## 1. Purpose

This document is a practical operational control for the PropFlo marketing team.

It is not legal advice.

It records what the marketing team needs from PropFlo before campaigns run, what each advertising platform requires, which claims may or may not be used, and which disclosures must appear where.

Where the product structure, the legal classification, or a specific claim has not been confirmed, this document records the item as pending rather than presenting it as fact.

### 4.3 Prohibited or High-Risk Claims

These claims must not be used in any PropFlo advertising, landing page, social post, brochure, email, or WhatsApp message.

| Claim | Reason |
|---|---|
| Guaranteed approval | Not truthful. Every finance product has qualification criteria. |
| Guaranteed cash | Not truthful. Payout depends on approval and process. |
| No credit checks | Likely inconsistent with any responsible finance model and platform policies. |
| Everybody qualifies | Not truthful. |
| Pre-approved finance | Not truthful without an actual pre-approval process. |
| Cheapest bridging finance | Not verifiable and creates comparative risk. |
| Blacklisted applicants welcome | High regulatory and reputational risk. |
| Free credit | Misleading. |
| Same-day payout as a general claim | Depends on process. May be usable only as a conditional claim with evidence. |
| Instant cash | Misleading. |

## 6. Campaign Approval Workflow

Every advertising campaign, landing page update, offer page change, promotional social post, brochure, email campaign, and WhatsApp broadcast follows this workflow before it goes live.

1. Strategy
2. Copy draft
3. Creative draft
4. Internal compliance check
5. Client product accuracy review
6. Legal review where required
7. Client sign-off
8. Platform submission
9. Launch
10. Monitoring
11. Archive approved version

## 7. Ownership and Review

- The marketing team owns this document as an operational control.
- PropFlo owns the underlying product, legal, and factual accuracy inputs.
- Legal review is required where the product classification, referral remuneration, or a specific claim is not clearly supported.
- The matrix is reviewed monthly as part of the monthly report and updated whenever a claim, disclosure, or platform status changes.

## 8. Disclaimer

Marketing compliance support does not replace formal legal advice.

Where interpretation of the underlying financial product, applicable legislation, or referral structure is required, confirmation must be obtained from PropFlo's appointed legal adviser.
