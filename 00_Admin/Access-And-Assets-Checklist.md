# PropFlo Access And Assets Checklist

## Purpose

This document lists the access, assets, and information Interon needs from PropFlo before implementation can begin.

The goal is to avoid delays after the proposal is approved.

## 1. Main Principle

Do not wait until launch week to request access.

Access and assets should be collected immediately after proposal approval.

## 2. Brand Assets Required

PropFlo should provide:

- Logo files
- Logo variations
- Brand colours
- Font names
- Existing brand guide, if available
- Existing brochures
- Existing pitch decks
- Existing adverts
- Existing social media graphics
- Existing photography or illustrations
- Existing icons or design assets

## 3. Website Access Required

Access may be required to:

- Website CMS
- Website hosting
- Domain or DNS
- Website forms
- Website database, if applicable
- Website plugin or integration settings
- SSL or security settings, if applicable

## 4. Analytics And Tracking Access Required

Access may be required to:

- Google Analytics 4
- Google Tag Manager
- Google Search Console
- Google Ads
- Meta Pixel or Events Manager
- LinkedIn Insight Tag, if used
- Call tracking system, if used
- CRM or lead tracking platform

## 5. Advertising Platform Access Required

Access may be required to:

- Google Ads account
- Meta Business Manager
- Facebook Page
- Instagram account
- LinkedIn Company Page
- LinkedIn Campaign Manager
- Google Business Profile, if used

## 6. CRM And Lead Management Access Required

Access may be required to:

- CRM system
- Lead spreadsheet
- Email notification system
- Sales pipeline
- WhatsApp Business account
- Email marketing platform
- Automation platform, if used

## 7. Legal And Compliance Documents Required

PropFlo should provide:

- Legal entity name
- Company registration number
- VAT number, if applicable
- Finance provider details
- NCR registration details, if applicable
- Required disclaimers
- Privacy policy
- Website terms and conditions
- Product terms
- Approved fee wording
- Approved repayment wording
- Approved qualification wording
- Approved approval and payout wording

## 8. Sales Process Information Required

PropFlo should confirm:

- Who receives leads
- Who contacts leads
- Working hours
- Response time target
- Lead qualification questions
- Lead stages
- Sales process
- Application process
- Document request process
- Approval process
- Decline process
- Funded deal reporting process

## 9. Contact Details Required

PropFlo should provide:

- Main business phone number
- Main email address
- WhatsApp number
- Sales contact
- Compliance contact
- Marketing contact
- Technical contact
- Emergency contact for campaign issues
