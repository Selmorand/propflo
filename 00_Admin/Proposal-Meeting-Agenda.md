# PropFlo Proposal Meeting Agenda

## Purpose

This document gives Interon a clear agenda for the PropFlo proposal meeting.

The goal is to keep the meeting focused, confirm the important details, and avoid discussing campaigns before the product, compliance, budget, and lead process are clear.

## 1. Meeting Details

### Date

To be confirmed.

### Time

To be confirmed.

### Attendees

To be confirmed.

### Meeting Owner

Interon.

## 2. Meeting Objective

The objective of this meeting is to confirm whether PropFlo is ready to proceed with a structured 90-day marketing launch.

The meeting should confirm:

- Business goals
- Product details
- Compliance requirements
- Budget range
- Website and landing page needs
- Lead handling process
- Reporting requirements
- Next steps

## 3. Agenda Item: Business Goal

### Discussion Points

- What does PropFlo want to achieve in the first 90 days?
- What does PropFlo want to achieve in the next 12 months?
- Which seller types are most valuable?
- Which geographic areas should be targeted?
- What does a successful funded deal look like?

### Output Required

Clear campaign goal and target audience.

## 4. Agenda Item: Product And Qualification

### Discussion Points

- What exact finance product is being marketed?
- Who qualifies?
- Who does not qualify?
- What documents are required?
- What is the process from enquiry to funding?
- What must happen before an application can proceed?

### Output Required

Confirmed product and qualification notes.

## 5. Agenda Item: Legal And Compliance

### Discussion Points

- Who provides final approval on wording?
- What disclaimers must be used?
- What claims may not be used?
- Are timing claims allowed?
- Are fee claims allowed?
- Are approval claims allowed?
- Are there platform verification requirements?

### Output Required

Confirmed approval process and compliance requirements.

## 6. Agenda Item: Website And Landing Pages

### Discussion Points

- Does PropFlo have an existing website?
- Does the website need improvement?
- Should we build campaign landing pages?
- What pages must be ready before ads launch?
- What form fields should be used?
- What privacy and consent wording is required?

### Output Required

Landing page and form direction.

## 7. Agenda Item: Paid Media

### Discussion Points

- Is Google Search Ads approved as the first channel?
- What monthly ad spend is acceptable?
- Should Meta retargeting be used?
- Should LinkedIn be used for partner marketing?
- Are there any channels PropFlo does not want to use?

### Output Required

Confirmed first campaign channels and starting ad budget.

## 8. Agenda Item: Lead Handling

### Discussion Points

- Who receives leads?
- Who contacts leads?
- How fast can leads be contacted?
- Where will leads be tracked?
- What lead stages should be used?
- How will Interon receive feedback on lead quality?
- Who reports funded deals back to Interon?

### Output Required

Confirmed lead handover process.

## 9. Agenda Item: Partner Marketing

### Discussion Points

- Will estate agents be targeted?
- Will conveyancers be targeted?
- Will bond originators be targeted?
- Are referral fees or commissions part of the model?
- What may partners say?
- What may partners not say?

### Output Required

Confirmed partner marketing direction.

## 10. Agenda Item: Budget

### Discussion Points

- Once-off setup cost
- Landing page or website cost
- Monthly management cost
- Suggested ad spend
- Third-party costs
- Minimum term
- Review point after 90 days

### Output Required

Budget agreement or revision notes.

## 11. Agenda Item: Risks And Limitations

### Discussion Points

- Marketing cannot guarantee funded deals.
- Platform approval is not guaranteed.
- Lead quality depends on targeting and offer clarity.
- Sales response time affects conversion.
- Tracking may not capture every offline conversion.
- Legal and compliance wording must be approved by PropFlo.

### Output Required

Client understands responsibilities and limitations.

## 12. Agenda Item: Next Steps

### Discussion Points

- What must Interon do next?
- What must PropFlo provide next?
- Who approves the proposal?
- What is the target start date?
- What is the target launch date?

### Output Required

Clear action list.
