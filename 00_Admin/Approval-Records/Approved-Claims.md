# PropFlo Approved Claims

## Purpose

This document records the wording PropFlo has approved for public marketing use.

Because PropFlo operates in a finance-related space, all claims about the service, approval, timing, fees, repayment, qualification, and process must be approved before use.

## 1. Main Rule

Only wording listed as approved in this document should be used publicly.

If wording is not listed here, it should not be used until PropFlo approves it.

## 7. Timing Claims

Do not use timing claims until approved.

Examples requiring approval:

- Same-day approval
- Funds within 24 hours
- Fast approval
- Quick payout
- Immediate assistance
- Urgent funding

## 8. Fee Claims

Do not use fee claims until approved.

Examples requiring approval:

- No upfront fee
- No hidden costs
- Low cost
- Affordable finance
- Fixed fee
- Transparent fees

## 9. Approval Claims

Do not use approval claims until approved.

Avoid:

- Guaranteed approval
- Everyone qualifies
- No checks
- No documents needed
- Instant approval
- You are approved
