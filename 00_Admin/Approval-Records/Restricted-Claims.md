# PropFlo Restricted Claims

## Purpose

This document lists marketing claims, phrases, and wording that should not be used unless PropFlo has specifically approved them.

Because PropFlo operates in a finance-related space, risky wording can create compliance, trust, platform approval, or client expectation problems.

## 1. Main Rule

If a claim creates an expectation about approval, timing, cost, repayment, qualification, risk, or outcome, it must be approved before use.

When in doubt, do not use the claim.

## 2. Approval Claims To Avoid

Avoid:

- Guaranteed approval
- Everyone qualifies
- You are approved
- Approval is certain
- No application can be declined
- No checks required
- No verification required
- No documents needed
- Instant approval

Safer alternative:

Subject to qualification, verification, approval, and PropFlo's terms and conditions.

## 3. Timing Claims To Avoid

Avoid unless approved:

- Instant payout
- Cash today
- Same-day payout
- Same-day approval
- Funds within 24 hours
- Immediate funding
- Urgent cash now
- Get money now
- Fast cash
- Quick cash

Safer alternative:

A PropFlo consultant can explain the process and next steps.

## 4. Cost Claims To Avoid

Avoid unless approved:

- No cost
- Free finance
- No fees
- No upfront cost
- No hidden costs
- Cheapest bridging finance
- Lowest fees
- Interest-free
- Risk-free
- No repayment pressure

Safer alternative:

Fees, repayment, and terms must be confirmed by PropFlo.

## 5. Risk Claims To Avoid

Avoid:

- Risk-free
- No risk
- Nothing can go wrong
- Safe for everyone
- Guaranteed solution
- Stress-free guarantee
- No consequences
- This will not affect your sale

Safer alternative:

PropFlo will review your enquiry and explain the next step, subject to qualification and verification.

## 6. Emotional Pressure Claims To Avoid

Avoid:

- Desperate for cash?
- Need money urgently?
- Don't wait another day
- Solve your money problems now
- Get cash before it is too late
- Avoid financial disaster
- Stop struggling today
- You cannot afford to wait

Safer alternative:

Property transfer can take time. PropFlo helps qualifying sellers explore whether bridging finance may be an option.

## 7. Property Process Claims To Avoid

Avoid unless approved:

- Transfer will not be affected
- The attorney will approve it
- Your sale is guaranteed
- The buyer cannot cancel
- The transfer cannot fail
- Repayment is automatic
- The conveyancer will handle everything

Safer alternative:

The process depends on the property transaction, required verification, and PropFlo's terms.

## 8. Partner Claims To Avoid

Partners should not say:

- I can get you approved
- PropFlo will pay you today
- You definitely qualify
- There are no costs
- You do not need documents
- This is guaranteed
- You do not need to read the terms
- This will not affect the transaction

Safer alternative:

I can introduce you to PropFlo so their team can explain the process and check whether you may qualify.

## 9. Platform Risk Notes

Advertising platforms may reject or restrict ads that use aggressive financial wording.

Higher-risk wording includes:

- guaranteed
- instant
- cash now
- no checks
- no documents
- no risk
- urgent money
- bad credit
- blacklisted
- debt rescue
- payday loan

## 10. Restricted Claims Table

| Restricted Claim | Risk | Safer Alternative | Approval Status |
|---|---|---|---|
| Guaranteed approval | Approval cannot be promised | Subject to qualification and approval | Not approved |
| Instant cash | Timing may vary | A consultant can explain the next step | Not approved |
| No checks | Verification is required | Subject to verification | Not approved |
| No documents needed | Documents may be required | Documents may be requested | Not approved |
| Risk-free | Financial products carry conditions | Terms and conditions apply | Not approved |
| Cash today | Timing must be confirmed | Process and timing depend on approval | Not approved |

## 11. Starting Recommendation

Use this document during every copy review.

Before publishing any advert, landing page, email, WhatsApp message, or partner script, check that it does not include restricted wording.
