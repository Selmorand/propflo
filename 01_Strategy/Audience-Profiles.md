# PropFlo Audience Profiles

## Purpose

This document defines the main audiences PropFlo should market to.

The goal is to make sure each audience receives the right message, through the right channel, with the right call to action.

## 1. Main Principle

PropFlo has more than one audience.

A property seller needs a simple, reassuring message.

An estate agent needs a referral message.

A conveyancer needs a careful, professional message.

A bond originator or property professional needs a business relationship message.

Do not use the same wording for everyone.

## 2. Audience 1: Property Sellers

### Who They Are

People who have sold or are selling a property and may need access to funds before transfer is complete.

### Main Problem

They may be waiting for sale proceeds but need money before the transfer process is finished.

### Possible Needs

- Moving costs
- Rates or levy payments
- Repairs
- Bond or settlement-related costs
- Deposit on another property
- Business cash flow
- Personal cash-flow pressure

### Main Questions

- Do I qualify?
- Is this legitimate?
- What does it cost?
- How quickly can I get help?
- What documents are needed?
- What happens if transfer is delayed?
- How is repayment handled?
- Is my information safe?

### Best Channels

- Google Search Ads
- Seller landing page
- FAQ page
- SEO content
- WhatsApp follow-up
- Email follow-up
- Meta retargeting

### Best CTA

Check if you qualify

### Message Angle

Property sold but still waiting for transfer?

PropFlo helps qualifying sellers explore funding linked to their property sale before transfer is complete.

Final wording must be approved by PropFlo.

## 3. Audience 2: Estate Agents

### Who They Are

Estate agents, estate agency principals, and property sales consultants who work directly with sellers.

### Main Problem

They may have sellers under financial pressure during the transfer period and may not know how to guide them safely.

### Possible Needs

- A clear referral process
- Approved wording
- Fast contact with the seller
- Confidence that the seller will be handled professionally
- No risk of making unapproved finance promises
- A way to support sellers without becoming responsible for finance advice

### Main Questions

- Which sellers can I refer?
- What may I say?
- What must I not say?
- Will PropFlo contact the seller directly?
- Are referral fees available?
- Will this affect the property transaction?
- How do I introduce PropFlo?

### Best Channels

- LinkedIn
- Direct email outreach
- WhatsApp outreach
- Estate agent partner page
- Partner PDF
- Referral network building

### Best CTA

Request partner information

### Message Angle

Help sellers understand their options while they wait for transfer.

PropFlo helps qualifying property sellers explore bridging finance linked to their property sale before transfer is complete.

Final wording must be approved by PropFlo.

## 4. Audience 3: Conveyancers

### Who They Are

Conveyancing attorneys, transfer attorneys, conveyancing secretaries, and property transfer professionals.

### Main Problem

They may deal with sellers who are waiting for transfer and may need funding, but they must avoid giving financial advice or making promises.

### Possible Needs

- Clear process information
- Professional boundaries
- Consent-aware information sharing
- Accurate documentation requirements
- Approved wording
- No sales pressure
- No implication that the conveyancer is arranging finance

### Main Questions

- What does PropFlo do?
- What information may be required?
- Does the seller deal directly with PropFlo?
- What should we avoid saying?
- What consent is required?
- Is there a formal process?
- Who do we contact?

### Best Channels

- LinkedIn
- Professional email outreach
- Conveyancer information page
- Referral meetings
- Partner information pack

### Best CTA

Request information

### Message Angle

Clear information for property transfer professionals.

PropFlo helps qualifying property sellers explore bridging finance linked to their property sale before transfer is complete.

Final wording must be approved by PropFlo.

- Approved wording

### Main Questions

- Can I refer sellers?
- What information is required?
- What may I say?
- Are referral arrangements available?
- How does the process work?

### Best Channels

- LinkedIn

- Referral relationship building

### Best CTA

Request partner information

### Message Angle

A practical funding option for qualifying property sellers waiting for transfer.

Final wording must be approved by PropFlo.

## 6. Audience 5: Property Developers And Investors

### Who They Are

People or companies involved in property transactions where timing and cash flow matter.

### Main Problem

They may have capital tied up in property transactions and need short-term flexibility.

### Possible Needs

- Clear funding process
- Fast assessment
- Professional communication
- Larger transaction handling
- Repeat relationship potential

### Main Questions

- What transaction types are supported?
- What amounts are possible?
- What documents are needed?
- How is repayment handled?
- Can this support repeat transactions?
- Who should we speak to?

### Best Channels

- LinkedIn
- Direct outreach
- Referral relationships
- Professional networking
- Website enquiry

### Best CTA

Speak to PropFlo

### Message Angle

Funding linked to qualifying property sale transactions.

Final wording must be approved by PropFlo.

## 7. Audience Priority

### Priority 1

Property sellers

Reason:

This is the primary direct lead source.

### Priority 2

Estate agents

Reason:

They can become repeat referral sources.

### Priority 3

Conveyancers

Reason:

They add trust and transaction-stage understanding, but require careful communication.

### Priority 4

Bond originators

Reason:

They may become useful professional referral partners.

### Priority 5

Developers and investors

Reason:

Potentially valuable but should be approached after the core offer is proven.

## 8. Messaging Rules By Audience

### Sellers

Use simple, reassuring language.

Focus on:

- Problem
- Process
- Qualification
- Next step

### Estate Agents

Use practical partner language.

Focus on:

- Referral process
- Approved wording
- Seller support
- Professional handling

### Conveyancers

Use formal, careful language.

Focus on:

- Information
- Boundaries
- Process
- Consent

### Bond Originators

Use professional relationship language.

Focus on:

- Client fit
- Referral process
- Property finance timing

### Developers And Investors

Use commercial language.

Focus on:

- Transaction timing
- Cash flow
- Repeat use
- Professional handling
