# PropFlo Competitor Research

## Purpose

This document defines how competitor research should be captured for PropFlo.

The goal is to understand how other providers position property bridging finance, what claims they use, what pages they rank for, and where PropFlo can differentiate.

## 1. Main Principle

Competitor research should not be used to copy competitors.

It should be used to understand:

- Market language
- Common offers
- Trust signals
- Qualification wording
- Fee visibility
- Landing page structure
- SEO opportunities
- Ad positioning
- Gaps PropFlo can use

## 2. Competitor Research Questions

For each competitor, capture:

- Who are they?
- What do they offer?
- Who do they target?
- What wording do they use?
- What claims do they make?
- What disclaimers do they show?
- What CTAs do they use?
- What pages do they have?
- Do they target sellers, agents, conveyancers, or all three?
- What trust signals do they use?
- What seems strong?
- What seems weak?
- What can PropFlo do differently?

## 4. Website Review Checklist

For each competitor website, check:

- Homepage message
- Bridging finance page
- Seller page
- Agent or partner page
- FAQ page
- Contact form
- Application form
- Disclaimers
- Privacy policy
- Terms and conditions
- Blog or educational content
- Testimonials
- Case studies
- Registration or compliance details

## 5. Message Review

Capture common phrases competitors use.

Examples to look for:

- Property bridging finance
- Seller bridging finance
- Advance on sale proceeds
- Access funds before transfer
- Bridging finance for property sellers
- Transfer finance
- Sale proceeds advance

## 6. Offer Review

Capture:

- Minimum amount
- Maximum amount
- Percentage of sale proceeds offered
- Approval timing
- Payout timing
- Fees
- Repayment method
- Qualification criteria
- Required documents

Only record information that is publicly available or provided by PropFlo.

## 7. SEO Review

Capture competitor pages that rank or appear useful.

Record:

- Page title
- Meta description
- URL
- Main topic
- Search intent
- Content quality
- FAQ coverage
- Internal links
- CTA quality

## 8. Paid Ads Review

If competitor ads are visible, record:

- Headline
- Description
- CTA
- Landing page
- Offer angle
- Any claims used
- Any disclaimers shown

## 9. Trust Signal Review

Look for:

- Legal entity details
- Registration numbers
- Industry memberships
- Testimonials
- Reviews
- Process explanation
- FAQ clarity
- Privacy policy
- Contact details
- Physical address
- Human support
- Professional partner material

## 10. Gap Analysis

Record gaps PropFlo can use.

Possible gaps:

- Competitors use confusing finance language.
- Competitors do not explain the process clearly.
- Competitors focus only on sellers, not partners.
- Competitors do not have strong FAQ content.
- Competitors lack clear trust signals.
- Competitors use aggressive loan-style copy.
- Competitors do not explain professional referral boundaries.

## 11. PropFlo Differentiation Ideas

Possible differentiation angles:

- Clear process
- Careful, professional wording
- Seller education
- Partner-safe referral material
- Strong FAQ content
- Trust-first landing pages
- Clear qualification explanation
- No hype or guaranteed approval wording
- Fast but responsible enquiry handling
