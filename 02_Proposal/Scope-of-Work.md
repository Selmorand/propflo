# PropFlo Scope of Work

## Purpose

This document defines what Interon will do for PropFlo during the marketing setup, launch, and monthly management process.

It is written as a working scope that can later be turned into a client proposal.

## Phase 1: Discovery and Strategy

### Objective

Understand the PropFlo business, product, audience, sales process, legal limitations, and growth targets before marketing activity begins.

### Work Included

- Discovery meeting with PropFlo
- Review of business model
- Review of finance product
- Review of target audiences
- Review of current website and online presence
- Review of sales and lead handling process
- Review of competitor positioning
- Review of required compliance wording
- Initial marketing strategy
- Initial channel recommendation
- Initial 90-day launch plan

### Output

- Marketing strategy document
- Audience and channel direction
- List of required client information
- Initial campaign structure

## Phase 2: Compliance and Claims Preparation

### Objective

Make sure all public-facing marketing claims are accurate, approved, and safe to use.

### Work Included

- Compile compliance questions
- Identify claims that require approval
- Prepare approved claims list
- Prepare restricted claims list
- Prepare required disclaimer list
- Prepare landing page wording notes
- Prepare ad wording notes

### Output

- Approved claims list
- Restricted claims list
- Disclaimer requirements
- Client sign-off record

### Important Note

Interon does not provide legal, financial, or regulatory compliance approval.

PropFlo must approve all legal, financial, fee, repayment, approval, qualification, and timing claims before publication.

## Phase 3: Website and Landing Page Planning

### Objective

Create the structure needed to convert traffic into qualified leads.

### Work Included

- Landing page structure
- Seller landing page plan
- Estate agent partner page plan
- Conveyancer partner page plan
- FAQ page plan
- Contact or application form plan
- Thank-you page plan
- Call-to-action structure
- Trust signal recommendations
- Basic SEO page structure

### Output

- Landing page sitemap
- Landing page content brief
- Form and lead capture recommendations
- Website conversion recommendations

## Phase 4: Tracking and Reporting Setup

### Objective

Ensure marketing performance can be measured from first click through to qualified lead and funded deal where possible.

### Work Included

- Tracking plan
- UTM structure
- Google Analytics 4 setup guidance
- Google Tag Manager setup guidance
- Google Ads conversion tracking plan
- Meta Pixel or retargeting tracking plan, if used
- Call and WhatsApp tracking recommendations
- Lead source tracking structure
- Monthly reporting template

### Output

- Tracking plan
- KPI dashboard structure
- Monthly report format
- Lead source tracking system

## Phase 5: Paid Campaign Setup

### Objective

Prepare controlled paid media campaigns that can be launched, tested, and optimised.

### Work Included

- Google Search Ads campaign structure
- Keyword research
- Negative keyword list
- Ad copy drafts
- Campaign budget recommendation
- Meta retargeting structure, if applicable
- LinkedIn partner awareness structure, if applicable
- Campaign testing plan

### Output

- Google Ads campaign plan
- Meta retargeting plan
- LinkedIn partner campaign plan
- Campaign launch checklist

## Phase 6: Content and SEO Setup

### Objective

Build trust, improve organic visibility, and support the paid advertising funnel.

### Work Included

- SEO topic plan
- FAQ content plan
- Blog topic plan
- Educational content plan
- Internal linking recommendations
- Search intent mapping
- Basic content calendar

### Output

- SEO content plan
- Blog topic list
- FAQ content structure
- 3-month content calendar

## Phase 7: Partner Marketing Setup

### Objective

Create a structured referral approach for estate agents, conveyancers, bond originators, and property professionals.

### Work Included

- Partner audience definition
- Partner value proposition
- Referral process outline
- Partner email template
- Partner WhatsApp message template
- Partner brochure or one-page brief outline
- LinkedIn outreach structure

### Output

- Partner outreach plan
- Partner message pack
- Referral process recommendation

## Phase 8: Launch and Optimisation

### Objective

Launch campaigns in a controlled way and improve based on real data.

### Work Included

- Campaign launch support
- Weekly search term review
- Lead quality review
- Budget pacing review
- Landing page improvement recommendations
- Ad copy testing
- Campaign optimisation
- Monthly report

### Output

- Weekly optimisation notes
- Monthly campaign report
- Next-month recommendations

## Phase 9: Monthly Marketing Management

### Objective

Manage the marketing system as an ongoing growth process.

### Work Included

- Paid campaign monitoring
- Budget recommendations
- SEO content coordination
- Partner campaign support
- Monthly reporting
- Lead quality review
- Conversion improvement recommendations
- Monthly strategy meeting

### Output

- Monthly report
- Campaign optimisation log
- Recommended next actions
- Updated budget recommendations

## Exclusions Unless Quoted Separately

The following are not included unless specifically quoted:

- Full website rebuild
- Full brand identity design
- Full CRM implementation
- Legal compliance review
- Financial product compliance advice
- Copy translation
- Video production
- Photography
- Advanced marketing automation
- Call centre or sales team management
- Ad spend
- Software subscriptions
- Third-party platform fees

## Client Responsibilities

PropFlo must provide:

- Business and legal information
- Product details
- Fee and repayment information
- Qualification criteria
- Required disclaimers
- Privacy policy and terms
- Sales process details
- Lead handling contacts
- Approval of all public-facing claims
- Feedback on lead quality
- Access to required platforms where needed

## Recommended Starting Scope

The recommended starting scope is a 90-day controlled launch.

Month 1:
Foundation, compliance, strategy, landing page planning, tracking, and campaign setup.

Month 2:
Launch Google Search Ads, retargeting if applicable, SEO content, and partner outreach.

Month 3:
Optimisation, reporting, lead quality improvement, and controlled budget scaling.
