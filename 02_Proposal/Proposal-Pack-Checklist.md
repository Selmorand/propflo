# PropFlo Proposal Pack Checklist

## Purpose

This document defines which files should be included or reviewed before creating the final client-facing proposal pack.

The goal is to make sure the proposal is complete, professional, and supported by the working documents already created.

## 1. Main Principle

Do not send every internal working file to the client.

The internal files are used to prepare the proposal.

The client-facing proposal should be shorter, clearer, and focused on:

- What Interon will do
- What PropFlo must provide
- What it will cost
- How the first 90 days will work
- What the limitations are
- What happens next

## 2. Internal Files To Review Before Proposal

Review these before finalising the proposal:

- 00_Admin\Compliance-Questions.md
- 00_Admin\Client-Questions-For-Proposal-Meeting.md
- 00_Admin\Access-And-Assets-Checklist.md
- 01_Strategy\Marketing-Strategy.md
- 01_Strategy\Channel-Plan.md
- 02_Proposal\Scope-of-Work.md
- 02_Proposal\Budget-Proposal.md
- 02_Proposal\Assumptions-and-Limitations.md
- 02_Proposal\Implementation-Plan.md
- 02_Proposal\Launch-Checklist.md
- 02_Proposal\Proposal-Draft.md

## 3. Client-Facing Proposal Sections

The final proposal should include:

1. Cover page
2. Executive summary
3. Business challenge
4. Recommended 90-day approach
5. Channel strategy
6. Scope of work
7. Implementation plan
8. Budget
9. Client responsibilities
10. Assumptions and limitations
11. Approval requirements
12. Next steps

## 4. Client-Facing Attachments

Possible attachments:

- 90-day launch plan
- Budget table
- Scope of work
- Assumptions and limitations
- Compliance questions
- Access and assets checklist

## 5. Internal Files Not Usually Sent To Client

These are usually internal working files unless the client asks for them:

- Optimisation logs
- Draft ad copy
- Negative keyword lists
- Internal content plans
- Internal lead tracking structure
- Raw meeting notes
- Internal creative notes

## 6. Proposal Review Checklist

Before sending, check:

- Pricing is correct
- VAT status is clear
- Ad spend is separate
- Third-party costs are separate
- Minimum term is clear
- Deliverables are clear
- Exclusions are clear
- Client responsibilities are clear
- Compliance responsibility is clear
- No guaranteed results are promised
- No unapproved finance claims are included
- Payment terms are included
- Next step is clear

## 7. Recommended Proposal Position

Recommended starting position:

- Once-off setup: R55,000 ex VAT
- Landing page set: R35,000 ex VAT
- Monthly management: R32,000 ex VAT
- Suggested ad spend: R25,000 to R40,000 per month
- Minimum term: 3 months
- Review point: End of month 3

## 8. Optional Lower-Cost Position

If PropFlo needs a smaller starting option:

- Once-off setup: R45,000 ex VAT
- Basic landing page: R15,000 ex VAT
- Monthly management: R18,000 ex VAT
- Suggested ad spend: R15,000 to R25,000 per month
- Minimum term: 3 months

## 9. Optional Higher-Growth Position

If PropFlo wants a stronger launch:

- Once-off setup: R65,000 ex VAT
- Landing page set: R45,000 ex VAT
- Monthly management: R55,000 ex VAT
- Suggested ad spend: R40,000 to R80,000 per month
- Minimum term: 3 months

## 10. Starting Recommendation

Use the proposal draft as the base.

Then refine it after the proposal meeting once PropFlo confirms:

- Product details
- Compliance wording
- Budget appetite
- Sales process
- Lead handling capacity
- Approval process
