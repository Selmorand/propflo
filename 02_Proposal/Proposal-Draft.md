# PropFlo Marketing Proposal Draft

## Purpose

This document is the working draft for the PropFlo marketing proposal.

It will be refined into a client-facing proposal after PropFlo confirms the product details, compliance wording, approval claims, budget appetite, and implementation timeline.

## 1. Proposal Title

PropFlo 90-Day Marketing Launch Proposal

## 2. Prepared For

PropFlo

## 3. Prepared By

Interon

## 4. Proposal Summary

PropFlo requires a structured marketing system to generate, track, qualify, and improve leads for property seller bridging finance.

This proposal recommends a controlled 90-day launch that includes strategy, compliance preparation, landing page planning, tracking, paid media setup, content planning, referral partner structure, and monthly optimisation.

The goal is not to simply run adverts.

The goal is to build a repeatable client acquisition system that can be measured, improved, and scaled.

## 5. Business Opportunity

Property sellers may experience a funding gap between accepting an offer and receiving sale proceeds after transfer.

PropFlo can help qualifying sellers explore bridging finance linked to their property sale before transfer is complete.

Final wording must be approved by PropFlo before public use.

## 6. Recommended Approach

The recommended approach is a controlled 90-day launch.

### Month 1: Foundation

- Confirm product and compliance details
- Confirm approved claims and disclaimers
- Finalise strategy
- Plan landing pages
- Prepare tracking
- Prepare campaign structure
- Prepare content and partner framework

### Month 2: Launch

- Launch Google Search Ads with controlled budget
- Launch retargeting if approved
- Start SEO content
- Start partner outreach
- Review lead quality weekly
- Improve landing page and form wording

### Month 3: Optimise

- Refine campaigns
- Add negative keywords
- Improve landing pages
- Improve lead handling
- Review partner response
- Review cost per qualified lead
- Prepare scale recommendation

## 7. Recommended Channels

The recommended starting channels are:

- Google Search Ads
- SEO and educational content
- Estate agent referral marketing
- Conveyancer referral information
- Meta retargeting, if approved
- LinkedIn partner awareness
- Email and WhatsApp follow-up
- CRM or lead source tracking

## 8. Scope Summary

Interon will assist with:

- Strategy and planning
- Campaign structure
- Landing page planning
- Tracking and reporting structure
- Google Ads setup
- Retargeting planning
- SEO content planning
- Partner material planning
- Lead follow-up workflow
- Monthly optimisation and reporting

## 9. Important Limitations

Marketing can generate enquiries and leads, but it cannot guarantee:

- A specific number of qualified leads
- A specific number of applications
- A specific number of approvals
- A specific number of funded deals
- Platform approval of every advert
- Search ranking results
- Sales conversion outcomes

Campaign performance depends on:

- Product clarity
- Compliance approval
- Lead response time
- Offer competitiveness
- Landing page quality
- Tracking accuracy
- Sales feedback from PropFlo
