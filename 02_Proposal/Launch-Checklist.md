# PropFlo Launch Checklist

## Purpose

This document defines what must be checked before any PropFlo marketing campaign goes live.

The goal is to prevent wasted ad spend, broken tracking, poor lead handling, missing approvals, and compliance issues.

## 1. Main Launch Rule

Do not launch advertising until the checklist is complete.

A campaign should only go live when:

- Wording is approved
- Landing pages are ready
- Forms work
- Tracking works
- Lead notifications work
- PropFlo can respond to leads
- Campaign settings are checked
- Budget is approved

## 2. Compliance Approval

Confirm:

- Core offer wording approved
- Disclaimer wording approved
- Qualification wording approved
- Fee wording approved, if used
- Timing wording approved, if used
- Repayment wording approved, if used
- Google Ads copy approved
- Meta copy approved, if used
- LinkedIn copy approved, if used
- Email templates approved
- WhatsApp scripts approved
- Partner wording approved

## 3. Website And Landing Pages

Confirm:

- Seller landing page complete
- Check if you qualify form complete
- Thank-you page complete
- FAQ page complete
- Privacy policy linked
- Terms and conditions linked
- Disclaimer visible
- CTA buttons working
- Phone links working
- WhatsApp links working
- Email links working
- Mobile layout checked
- Page speed acceptable
- No unapproved claims visible

## 4. Forms

Confirm:

- Form submits correctly
- Required fields work
- Consent checkbox works
- Privacy wording included
- Confirmation message works
- Thank-you page loads
- PropFlo receives notification
- Lead data is saved
- Test lead captured correctly
- No unnecessary sensitive information is requested

## 5. Tracking

Confirm:

- Google Analytics 4 installed
- Google Tag Manager installed
- Google Search Console connected
- Google Ads account connected
- Google Ads conversion tracking active
- Form submission tracking active
- Thank-you page tracking active
- Phone click tracking active
- WhatsApp click tracking active
- UTM links tested
- Test conversions recorded
- Lead source captured

## 6. Lead Handling

Confirm:

- Lead owner assigned
- Lead notification recipient confirmed
- Response time agreed
- Lead stages agreed
- Follow-up workflow agreed
- Sales handover agreed
- Email confirmation ready
- WhatsApp response script ready
- Document checklist ready
- Lead tracker ready
- Weekly feedback process agreed

## 7. Google Ads

Confirm:

- Campaign name correct
- Location targeting correct
- Daily budget correct
- Keywords loaded
- Negative keywords loaded
- Match types checked
- Ad copy approved
- Final URLs correct
- UTM tracking added
- Conversion actions selected
- Bidding strategy checked
- Ad schedule checked, if used
- Extensions/assets checked
- No disallowed claims in ads

## 8. Meta Retargeting

Use this section only if Meta is used.

Confirm:

- Meta Business Manager access confirmed
- Pixel or tracking approved
- Audience created
- Retargeting audience checked
- Ad copy approved
- Creative approved
- CTA approved
- Destination URL checked
- UTM tracking added
- Financial services restrictions considered

## 9. LinkedIn

Use this section only if LinkedIn is used.

Confirm:

- LinkedIn Page access confirmed
- Partner audience defined
- Post copy approved
- Outreach message approved
- Partner CTA approved
- Destination URL checked
- UTM tracking added

## 10. Budget

Confirm:

- Setup fee approved
- Monthly management fee approved
- Ad spend approved
- Daily budget agreed
- Payment method confirmed
- Platform billing confirmed
- Budget owner confirmed
- Budget review date agreed

## 11. Final Pre-Launch Test

Complete one full test:

1. Visit seller landing page.
2. Click CTA.
3. Submit test form.
4. Confirm thank-you page loads.
5. Confirm email notification arrives.
6. Confirm lead appears in tracker or CRM.
7. Confirm GA4 event fires.
8. Confirm Google Ads conversion records, where possible.
9. Confirm lead source is visible.
10. Confirm PropFlo knows how to handle the lead.

## 13. First Week Monitoring

During the first week, check:

- Spend
- Search terms
- Lead quality
- Form submissions
- Tracking
- Notifications
- Sales response time
- Disapproved ads
- Irrelevant searches
- Not-qualified reasons

## 14. Starting Recommendation

Launch only Google Search Ads first unless PropFlo has approved additional channels.

Keep the first launch controlled so mistakes can be fixed before scaling.
