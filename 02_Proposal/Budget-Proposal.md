# PropFlo Budget Proposal

## Purpose

This document sets out suggested costs for the PropFlo marketing project.

It separates once-off setup costs, monthly management fees, media spend, third-party costs, and optional extras.

All pricing is a working recommendation and can be adjusted before the final client proposal.

## 1. Pricing Principles

The PropFlo project should not be priced as basic social media management.

This is a finance-related lead generation and marketing operations project.

The work includes strategy, compliance preparation, campaign setup, landing page planning, tracking, reporting, paid media management, content planning, partner marketing, and ongoing optimisation.

Ad spend must be separate from Interon's fees.

Third-party software and platform fees must also be separate unless specifically included.

## 11. Important Commercial Notes

Ad spend is not included in Interon's management fees.

Ad spend does not guarantee results.

Campaign performance depends on product clarity, compliance approval, landing page quality, lead response time, and offer competitiveness.

The first 90 days should be treated as a controlled launch and learning period.

After 90 days, the budget should be reviewed based on:

- Cost per qualified lead
- Cost per application
- Cost per approved application
- Cost per funded deal
- Lead quality by channel
- Sales team response time
- Revenue per funded deal
