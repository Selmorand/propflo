# PropFlo FAQ Page Brief

## Purpose

This document defines the content and layout brief for the PropFlo FAQ page.

The goal is to answer common questions clearly before sellers, estate agents, conveyancers, or referral partners make an enquiry.

## 1. Page Goal

Reduce uncertainty and improve trust.

The FAQ page should help visitors understand:

- What PropFlo does
- Who may qualify
- What information may be needed
- How the process works
- What approval depends on
- What happens after enquiry
- What partners should know

## 2. Target Audience

Primary audience:

- Property sellers

Secondary audience:

- Estate agents
- Conveyancers
- Bond originators
- Referral partners
- Family members helping sellers research options

## 3. Main Page Message

Suggested draft:

Answers to common questions about PropFlo, property seller bridging finance, and the enquiry process.

Final wording must be approved by PropFlo.
