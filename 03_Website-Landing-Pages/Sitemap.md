# PropFlo Landing Page Sitemap

## Purpose

This document defines the recommended landing page structure for PropFlo.

The goal is to separate direct seller leads from partner/referral leads so that each audience sees the right message.

## 1. Recommended Page Structure

The recommended initial page structure is:

1. Home or campaign overview page
2. Property seller landing page
3. Estate agent partner page
4. Conveyancer partner page
5. How it works page
6. FAQ page
7. Check if you qualify page
8. Thank-you page
9. Contact page

## 2. Priority Build Order

### Priority 1

These should be built first:

1. Property seller landing page
2. Check if you qualify page or form
3. Thank-you page
4. FAQ page

These are needed before Google Ads should launch.

### Priority 2

These should be built next:

1. Estate agent partner page
2. Conveyancer partner page
3. How it works page

These support referral and trust-building activity.

### Priority 3

These can come later:

1. Case studies
2. Blog articles
3. Partner resources
4. Downloadable guides
5. Calculator or qualification tool

## 3. Property Seller Landing Page

### Purpose

Convert property sellers into qualified enquiries.

### Target Audience

Property sellers who have sold or are selling a property and need access to funds before transfer is complete.

### Main Message

Property sold but still waiting for transfer?

PropFlo helps qualifying sellers access funds linked to their property sale before transfer is complete.

Final wording must be approved by PropFlo before use.

### Recommended Sections

1. Hero section
2. Problem explanation
3. How PropFlo helps
4. Who this is for
5. Who may qualify
6. What documents may be needed
7. How the process works
8. Costs and repayment explanation
9. Trust and compliance section
10. FAQ section
11. Main call to action

### Main CTA

Check if you qualify

### Secondary CTA

Speak to a consultant

## 4. Check If You Qualify Page or Form

### Purpose

Capture enough information to decide whether the seller may be a suitable lead.

### Recommended Fields

- Name
- Surname
- Email
- Cell number
- Preferred contact method
- Property location
- Has the property been sold?
- Has an Offer to Purchase been signed?
- Is a transferring attorney appointed?
- Estimated property sale value
- Estimated amount required
- Reason for needing funds
- Consent checkbox
- Privacy policy acknowledgement

### Important Note

The form should not ask for unnecessary sensitive information at the first step.

Sensitive financial or legal documents should only be requested through the approved client process.

## 5. Thank-You Page

### Purpose

Confirm the enquiry and explain what happens next.

### Recommended Message

Thank you. Your enquiry has been received.

A PropFlo consultant will review your information and contact you to discuss the next step.

### Recommended Sections

- Confirmation message
- Expected response time
- What to prepare
- Important disclaimer
- Contact details

## 6. FAQ Page

### Purpose

Reduce fear, answer common questions, and support SEO.

### Recommended Questions

1. What is property bridging finance?
2. Who qualifies for PropFlo?
3. Can I apply before transfer is complete?
4. Do I need an accepted Offer to Purchase?
5. What documents are needed?
6. How long does approval take?
7. How long does payout take?
8. What does it cost?
9. How is repayment handled?
10. What happens if transfer is delayed?
11. What happens if the sale falls through?
12. Is my personal information protected?

## 7. Estate Agent Partner Page

### Purpose

Help estate agents understand when and how to refer sellers.

### Target Audience

Estate agents and estate agency principals.

### Recommended Sections

1. Why sellers may need bridging finance
2. How PropFlo helps sellers
3. When to refer a seller
4. What the agent may say
5. What the agent should not promise
6. Referral process
7. Partner FAQ
8. Contact or partner registration form

### Main CTA

Become a referral partner

### Secondary CTA

Speak to PropFlo

## 8. Conveyancer Partner Page

### Purpose

Provide a careful, professional explanation for conveyancing attorneys and transfer professionals.

### Target Audience

Conveyancers, transfer attorneys, and legal/property professionals.

### Recommended Sections

1. Overview
2. Transaction-stage funding need
3. How PropFlo verifies transactions
4. Professional boundaries
5. Required documentation
6. Communication process
7. Compliance notes
8. Contact form

### Main CTA

Request information

### Secondary CTA

Speak to PropFlo

## 9. How It Works Page

### Purpose

Explain the process simply.

### Suggested Steps

1. Seller makes an enquiry.
2. PropFlo checks basic qualification.
3. Property sale details are reviewed.
4. Required documents are requested.
5. Transaction is verified.
6. Offer is confirmed.
7. Funds are paid if approved.
8. Repayment happens from the property transaction process.

Final wording must be confirmed by PropFlo.

## 10. Contact Page

### Purpose

Give users a simple way to contact PropFlo.

### Recommended Contact Options

- Phone
- Email
- WhatsApp
- Callback form
- Partner enquiry form

## 11. Important Website Notes

Each page must include:

- Clear CTA
- Approved disclaimer
- Privacy policy link
- POPIA consent where forms are used
- Trust information
- Simple language
- No unapproved finance claims

## 12. Starting Recommendation

Build these first:

1. Property seller landing page
2. Check if you qualify form
3. Thank-you page
4. FAQ page

Once those are working, build the partner pages.
