# PropFlo Estate Agent Partner Page Brief

## Purpose

This document defines the content and layout brief for the estate agent partner landing page.

The goal is to help estate agents understand when PropFlo may be relevant to sellers and how to refer enquiries safely.

## 1. Page Goal

Convert estate agents and estate agency principals into referral partners or professional contacts.

The page should explain:

- What PropFlo does
- Which sellers may need help
- When an estate agent should mention PropFlo
- What agents may say
- What agents must not promise
- How the referral process works

## 2. Target Audience

Primary audience:

- Estate agents
- Estate agency principals
- Property sales consultants
- Property franchise managers

Secondary audience:

- Bond originators
- Property consultants
- Referral partners

## 3. Main Page Message

Suggested draft:

Help sellers understand their options while they wait for transfer.

PropFlo helps qualifying property sellers explore bridging finance linked to their property sale before transfer is complete.

Final wording must be approved by PropFlo.

## 4. Hero Section

### Headline

A practical funding option for qualifying property sellers

### Supporting Text

Some sellers may need access to funds while waiting for transfer to complete. PropFlo helps qualifying sellers explore bridging finance linked to their property sale.

### Primary CTA

Request partner information

### Secondary CTA

Speak to PropFlo

### Disclaimer Line

Subject to qualification, verification, approval, and PropFlo's terms and conditions.

Final disclaimer must be approved by PropFlo.

## 5. Problem Section

### Heading

Sellers may need help before transfer is complete

### Content Notes

Explain that estate agents are often close to the seller and may notice when a seller has a timing or cash-flow issue.

Keep the wording careful.

Do not suggest that the agent is giving financial advice.

## 6. How PropFlo Helps Sellers

### Heading

How PropFlo may help your sellers

### Content Notes

Explain that PropFlo reviews the seller's situation and checks whether sale-linked bridging finance may be possible.

Do not promise approval, payout timing, or costs unless approved.

## 7. When To Refer A Seller

### Heading

When a seller may be suitable

Suggested bullets:

- The seller has accepted an offer.
- Transfer is still in progress.
- The seller may need access to funds before proceeds are paid.
- A transferring attorney has been appointed.
- The seller is willing to speak directly with PropFlo.

Final qualification wording must be approved by PropFlo.

## 8. What Agents May Say

### Heading

What you may say to sellers

Suggested wording:

PropFlo may be able to assist qualifying sellers who need access to funds linked to their property sale before transfer is complete.

If you would like, I can introduce you to PropFlo so they can explain the process and check whether you may qualify.

Final wording must be approved by PropFlo.

## 9. What Agents Must Not Promise

### Heading

What agents should not promise

Agents should not say:

- You are guaranteed to qualify.
- You will receive funds today.
- There are no costs.
- There are no checks.
- No documents are needed.
- The process is guaranteed.
- This will not affect your transaction.

## 10. Referral Process

### Heading

Simple referral process

Suggested steps:

1. Identify a seller who may need help.
2. Use approved wording to introduce PropFlo.
3. Seller agrees to be contacted.
4. PropFlo contacts the seller directly.
5. PropFlo checks basic qualification.
6. PropFlo manages the enquiry process.

Final process must be approved by PropFlo.

## 11. Partner FAQ Section

Include these questions:

1. Who can I refer?
2. What should I say to a seller?
3. What should I avoid saying?
4. Does the seller need a signed Offer to Purchase?
5. Does a transferring attorney need to be appointed?
6. Will PropFlo contact the seller directly?
7. Are referral fees available?
8. What happens if the seller does not qualify?
9. Can I use PropFlo wording in my own communication?
10. Who do I contact for partner information?

## 12. Final CTA Section

### Heading

Want to refer sellers safely and clearly?

### CTA

Request partner information

### Supporting Text

PropFlo can provide approved wording and a simple referral process for estate agents.

## 13. Design Notes

The page should feel:

- Professional
- Clear
- Partner-focused
- Trustworthy
- Practical
- Less sales-heavy than the seller page

Avoid:

- Aggressive commission messaging
- Over-promising to agents
- Finance guarantees
- Legal advice language
- Desperate seller language

## 14. Tracking Notes

Track:

- Page visit
- Partner CTA click
- Partner form submission
- Phone click
- WhatsApp click
- Download clicks, if partner PDF is added

## 15. Approval Notes

Before publishing, PropFlo must approve:

- Partner wording
- Referral process
- Referral fee wording, if any
- Seller introduction wording
- Disclaimer wording
- FAQ answers
- Contact process
