1. Project Summary

Structure: Two stages. Stage A (preparation) then Stage B (true 90-day live campaign). The 90-day clock starts at launch, not kick-off.
Calculated Stage A: 49 working days (~10 weeks; ~15 weeks pessimistic). Overall programme: ~159 calendar days (~5.2 months) to the Day-90 scale decision.
Critical path: compliance → landing-page copy & build → pages approval → tracking test → campaign build → pre-launch testing → launch.
Budget (ex VAT): R233,500–R283,500 across the overlap scenarios; R32,000/month management continuing after Day 90. Platform build separate.

Source of truth: CPM-PERT-MASTER-SCHEDULE.md, PROJECT-ACTIVITY-DATA.csv, PROJECT-GANTT-DATA.csv, PROJECT-COST-BASELINE.md.

14. Key Assumptions

  • Stage A is scheduled in working days from authorisation; Stage B in live days from launch. Calendar view uses 5 working days = 7 calendar days.
  • Durations use planning/PERT most-likely values and require PropFlo review; they are not confirmed facts.
  • No calendar dates are set; Day 0 = authorisation.
  • One Interon delivery team; no resource-levelling constraint applied.
  • Compliance answers, access, and approvals are provided promptly; delays move the launch date.
  • Marketing figures are ex VAT; platform-build figures are indicative and separate.
  • Brand name confirmed as PropFlo. Numeric lead/application/funded-deal targets to be agreed.
