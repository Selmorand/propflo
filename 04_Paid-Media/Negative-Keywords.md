# PropFlo Negative Keywords

## Purpose

This document lists search terms that should usually be excluded from PropFlo Google Ads campaigns.

The goal is to reduce wasted spend and avoid attracting people looking for unrelated loans, jobs, free help, or general finance information.

## 1. Main Principle

PropFlo should not pay for clicks from people looking for general loans.

The campaign should focus on property sellers who need bridging finance linked to a property sale.

- cash loan

- short term loan
- unsecured loan
- salary loan
- student loan

## 6. Legal Dispute Negative Keywords

Use carefully depending on PropFlo's actual service.

- divorce
- deceased estate
- insolvency
- sequestration
- repossession
- foreclosure
- eviction
- dispute
- court case
- litigation

These may not always be wrong, but they may create complicated or unsuitable leads.

## 7. Low-Quality Finance Intent

- no credit check
- guaranteed approval
- loan without documents
- loan without payslip
- loan without bank statement
- loan for blacklisted
- urgent cash now
- cash today
- money now

## 8. Competitor Negative Keywords

Do not automatically add competitor names as negatives.

Competitor searches can sometimes convert, but they can also be expensive and sensitive.

Create a separate competitor strategy later if needed.

## 9. Negative Keyword Match Type Notes

Use phrase match negatives for clear unwanted phrases.

Example:

"payday loan"

Use exact match negatives when the word may also appear in useful searches.

Example:

[free]

Be careful with single-word negatives because they can block useful traffic.

## 10. Search Term Review Process

During the first month, review search terms at least twice per week.

Add negatives when searches are clearly irrelevant.

Examples to exclude:

- people looking for employment
- people looking for personal loans
- people looking for car finance
- people looking for home loans
- people looking for rental property
- people looking for study material
- people looking for free templates

## 11. Starting Recommendation

Start with a conservative negative keyword list.

Do not block too aggressively before seeing real search term data.

The first month should be used to learn what people are actually searching.
