# PropFlo Google Ads Keywords

## Purpose

This document defines the starting keyword structure for PropFlo Google Search Ads.

The goal is to capture people actively searching for property bridging finance, seller finance, or access to funds before transfer.

## 1. Main Keyword Principle

Start narrow.

Do not begin with broad match keywords.

At launch, use exact match and phrase match so the campaign does not waste money on irrelevant finance searches.

## 2. Primary Keyword Themes

### Property Bridging Finance

- "property bridging finance"
- "property bridging finance south africa"
- "bridging finance property sale"
- "bridging finance for property sellers"
- "bridging finance before transfer"
- "bridging finance against property sale"

### Seller Finance

- "property seller finance"
- "seller bridging finance"
- "finance for property sellers"
- "seller advance finance"
- "advance on property sale"
- "advance on property proceeds"

### Transfer-Related Finance

- "money before property transfer"
- "cash before property transfer"
- "access money before transfer"
- "waiting for property transfer money"
- "property sold waiting for transfer"
- "funds before property transfer"

### Sale Proceeds

- "advance on sale proceeds"
- "property sale proceeds advance"
- "access sale proceeds early"
- "advance against sale proceeds"
- "get money before property transfer"

## 3. Partner Keywords

These should be tested separately from seller campaigns.

### Estate Agent Keywords

- "bridging finance for estate agents"
- "seller finance for estate agents"
- "property seller bridging finance referral"
- "estate agent seller finance"

### Conveyancer Keywords

- "bridging finance conveyancer"
- "property transfer bridging finance"
- "seller finance conveyancing"
- "bridging finance transfer attorney"

## 4. Keyword Match Type Recommendation

Use these match types at launch:

### Exact Match

Example:

[property bridging finance]

Use for the most important and most specific searches.

### Phrase Match

Example:

"property bridging finance"

Use for controlled variation.

### Avoid Broad Match Initially

Do not start with broad match because it may attract irrelevant searches such as:

- personal loans
- payday loans
- business loans
- bad credit loans
- home loans
- car finance
- debt consolidation

## 5. Suggested Initial Campaign Groups

### Campaign 1: Seller Search

Ad groups:

1. Property bridging finance
2. Seller advance finance
3. Transfer finance
4. Sale proceeds advance

### Campaign 2: Partner Search

Ad groups:

1. Estate agent referrals
2. Conveyancer referrals
3. Property professional referrals

### Campaign 3: Brand Search

Ad groups:

1. PropFlo brand
2. PropFlo bridging finance

Only use this if people are already searching for PropFlo by name.

## 6. High-Intent Keywords

Prioritise these first:

- "property bridging finance"
- "bridging finance for property sellers"
- "bridging finance before transfer"
- "cash before property transfer"
- "advance on property sale"
- "property sale proceeds advance"
- "access money before property transfer"

## 7. Lower-Intent Keywords

Use carefully or leave for later:

- "how does bridging finance work"
- "what is bridging finance"
- "how long does transfer take"
- "property transfer process"
- "selling a property in south africa"

These may be better for SEO content than paid ads.

## 8. Keywords To Avoid At Launch

Avoid keywords that are too broad, such as:

- loans
- cash loan
- personal loan
- payday loan
- quick loan
- instant loan
- business loan
- home loan
- debt help
- bad credit loan
- blacklisted loan

These may waste budget and attract poor-quality leads.

## 9. Location Notes

Campaign targeting should be confirmed with PropFlo.

Questions:

1. Is PropFlo national?
2. Are all provinces supported?
3. Are some cities preferred?
4. Are rural properties supported?
5. Are commercial properties supported?
6. Are agricultural properties supported?

## 10. Budget Notes

Start with a controlled daily budget.

Recommended starting ad spend:

R15,000 to R25,000 for the first month.

Increase only when lead quality is proven.

## 11. Launch Notes

Before launch, confirm:

- Approved claims
- Approved disclaimers
- Landing page complete
- Tracking tested
- Lead handling process ready
- Negative keywords loaded
- Conversion tracking active
- Client response process confirmed

## 12. Starting Recommendation

Start with one focused Google Search campaign for property sellers.

Do not launch too many campaigns at once.

Get clean data first, then expand.
