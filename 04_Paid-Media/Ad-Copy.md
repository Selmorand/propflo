# PropFlo Ad Copy

## Purpose

This document holds the draft advert copy for PropFlo paid media campaigns.

It covers Google Search Ads, Meta retargeting, and LinkedIn partner ads.

All copy here is a working draft. None of it is approved for public use yet.

## 1. Main Copy Principle

Ad copy must be clear, calm, and honest.

It must not overpromise.

It must match what PropFlo can actually deliver once the product and compliance details are confirmed.

Weak, unclear, or non-compliant copy wastes ad spend and attracts the wrong leads.

## 2. Compliance Status

Approval status:

Not yet approved.

Before any advert goes live, PropFlo must confirm:

- The core offer wording
- Qualification wording
- Fee and repayment wording
- Approval and payout timing wording
- Required disclaimers
- Any financial services advertising restrictions

Until then, treat every line below as a placeholder for confirmation.

## 3. Claims That May Not Be Used

Do not use the following unless explicitly approved and recorded in Approved-Claims.md:

- Guaranteed approval
- Instant cash
- Same-day payout
- No checks
- No documents needed
- No risk
- No fees
- Any specific approval time
- Any specific payout time
- Any minimum or maximum amount

If a claim is not approved, mark it as [TO CONFIRM] rather than presenting it as fact.

## 6. Meta Retargeting Ads

Meta ads are for retargeting people who visited the website but did not enquire.

Keep the tone reassuring, not urgent.

Do not use countdown timers, cash imagery, or pressure language.

### Primary Text Drafts

- Still waiting for your property transfer to complete? PropFlo helps qualifying sellers explore finance linked to their sale. Check if you may qualify.
- You looked into bridging finance for your property sale. When you are ready, PropFlo can help you explore your options. No pressure, just clear information.

### Headline Drafts

- Explore Sale-Linked Finance
- Waiting For Transfer?
- See If You May Qualify

### Description Drafts

- Simple enquiry for qualifying property sellers.
- Finance linked to your property sale, subject to qualification.

## 7. LinkedIn Partner Ads

LinkedIn ads target property professionals, not sellers directly.

Use formal, restrained, professional language.

### Primary Text Drafts

- Estate agents and conveyancers: some property sellers need access to funds while waiting for transfer to complete. PropFlo works with qualifying sellers to explore sale-linked bridging finance. Learn how referrals work.
- Property professionals often meet sellers facing a cash-flow gap before transfer. PropFlo offers a clear, professional enquiry process for qualifying sellers. Find out more about partnering.

### Headline Drafts

- Partner With PropFlo
- Seller Bridging Finance Referrals
- Support Sellers Awaiting Transfer

## 8. Disclaimer Placeholders

Every advert and landing page may require a disclaimer.

PropFlo must supply the approved disclaimer wording.

Placeholder examples, for confirmation only:

- Finance is subject to qualification and approval. [TO CONFIRM]
- Terms, conditions, and fees apply. [TO CONFIRM]
- PropFlo is [legal status to confirm]. [TO CONFIRM]

Do not publish any advert without the confirmed disclaimer.

## 9. Copy Testing Notes

When testing is approved, test one variable at a time:

- One message angle against another
- One headline set against another
- One call to action against another

Judge copy on qualified leads, not clicks.

Keep the best-performing approved variations and pause the rest.

## 10. Starting Recommendation

Do not finalise any advert until the offer, claims, and disclaimers are approved.

Start with the seller search campaign using the safest approved angle.

Keep the first adverts simple, honest, and clear, then improve them based on real lead quality.
