# PropFlo KPI Dashboard

## Purpose

This document defines the key performance indicators that should be tracked for PropFlo marketing.

The goal is to measure business value, not vanity metrics.

## 1. Main Reporting Principle

The most important metric is not clicks, impressions, or likes.

The most important metric is whether marketing produces qualified leads that become funded deals.

## 2. Traffic Metrics

Track:

- Website sessions
- Users
- New users
- Landing page visits
- Traffic source
- Traffic medium
- Campaign name
- Device type
- Engagement rate

## 3. Paid Media Metrics

Track:

- Impressions
- Clicks
- Click-through rate
- Cost per click
- Total spend
- Leads
- Cost per lead
- Qualified leads
- Cost per qualified lead
- Conversion rate
- Wasted spend

## 4. Google Ads Metrics

Track:

- Campaign spend
- Keyword performance
- Search terms
- Negative keywords added
- Ad copy performance
- Conversion actions
- Cost per enquiry
- Cost per qualified enquiry

## 5. Lead Metrics

Track:

- Total leads
- New leads
- Contacted leads
- No-answer leads
- Qualified leads
- Not-qualified leads
- Duplicate leads
- Spam leads
- Partner leads
- Seller leads

## 6. Sales Pipeline Metrics

Track:

- Applications started
- Documents requested
- Documents received
- Applications submitted
- Applications approved
- Applications declined
- Deals funded
- Deals lost
- Average funded value
- Revenue per funded deal, if shared by PropFlo

## 7. Cost Metrics

Track:

- Cost per lead
- Cost per qualified lead
- Cost per application
- Cost per approved application
- Cost per funded deal
- Total marketing spend
- Total ad spend
- Total management cost
- Estimated return on marketing spend, if revenue data is available

## 8. Partner Metrics

Track:

- Estate agent enquiries
- Conveyancer enquiries
- Bond originator enquiries
- Active referral partners
- Leads per referral partner
- Qualified leads per referral partner
- Funded deals per referral partner

## 9. Response Time Metrics

Track:

- Average time to first contact
- Leads contacted within 15 minutes
- Leads contacted within 1 hour
- Leads contacted same day
- Leads not contacted
- Follow-up attempts per lead

## 10. Content Metrics

Track:

- Blog posts published
- FAQ page visits
- Organic search visits
- Search Console impressions
- Search Console clicks
- Top search queries
- Top landing pages
- Content-assisted leads

## 11. Compliance and Platform Health

This is an operational monitoring section, not a lead metric. It sits alongside the main funnel metrics, not in place of them.

Track:

- Number of campaigns submitted
- Number approved first time
- Number rejected
- Rejection reason
- Average time from draft to approval
- Landing pages requiring changes
- Consent capture rate on forms
- Unsubscribe rate on email
- WhatsApp opt-in rate
- Complaints received
- Platform warnings received
- Account restrictions
- Partner referral consent completeness
- Expired or outdated claims requiring review

Detailed status and approvals are recorded in `00_Admin/Advertising-Compliance-Matrix.md` and `00_Admin/Approval-Records/Approval-Log.md`.

## 12. Monthly Report Questions

Each monthly report should answer:

1. How much was spent?
2. How many leads were generated?
3. How many leads were qualified?
4. Which channel produced the best leads?
5. Which channel wasted money?
6. Which keywords worked?
7. Which search terms should be excluded?
8. Which landing page converted best?
9. What objections or questions appeared repeatedly?
10. How quickly were leads contacted?
11. How many applications were started?
12. How many deals were funded?
13. What should change next month?

## 13. Monthly Summary Format

Use this simple structure:

- What happened
- What worked
- What did not work
- What we changed
- What we recommend next
- Budget recommendation
- Risks or blockers

## 14. Starting Recommendation

Do not report only on advertising platform data.

Advertising platforms can show clicks and conversions, but PropFlo must provide lead status updates so we can understand real business results.
