# PropFlo Tracking Plan

## Purpose

This document defines what must be tracked before PropFlo marketing campaigns go live.

The goal is to measure the full journey from advert click to qualified lead, application, approval, and funded deal where possible.

## 1. Main Tracking Principle

Clicks are not the final result.

The real goal is to understand which channels produce qualified leads and funded deals.

## 2. Core Tools Required

The recommended tracking setup includes:

- Google Analytics 4
- Google Tag Manager
- Google Search Console
- Google Ads conversion tracking
- Meta Pixel, if Meta retargeting is used
- UTM tracking
- Lead source tracking
- CRM or lead status tracking sheet

## 3. Website Events To Track

Track the following website actions:

- Form submission
- Thank-you page visit
- Phone number click
- WhatsApp click
- Email click
- Partner enquiry submission
- Qualification form start
- Qualification form completion
- FAQ page visit
- Landing page visit

## 4. Google Ads Conversions

Recommended Google Ads conversion actions:

- Seller enquiry form submitted
- Qualification form submitted
- Phone click
- WhatsApp click
- Partner enquiry submitted

Primary conversions:

- Seller enquiry form submitted
- Qualification form submitted

Secondary conversions:

- Phone click
- WhatsApp click
- Partner enquiry submitted

## 5. Lead Source Fields

Every lead should capture:

- Date received
- Name
- Contact number
- Email
- Source
- Medium
- Campaign
- Landing page
- Lead type
- Lead status
- Qualification status
- Application status
- Approval status
- Funded deal status
- Notes

## 6. Suggested Lead Statuses

Use these lead statuses:

1. New lead
2. Contacted
3. No answer
4. Not qualified
5. Qualified
6. Documents requested
7. Application submitted
8. Approved
9. Declined
10. Funded
11. Lost
12. Duplicate
13. Spam

## 7. UTM Structure

Use a consistent UTM structure for all campaign links.

Example:

utm_source=google
utm_medium=cpc
utm_campaign=property_seller_search
utm_content=ad_variant_1
utm_term=property_bridging_finance

Example channel values:

- google
- meta
- linkedin
- email
- whatsapp
- referral
- organic
- direct

Example medium values:

- cpc
- organic
- social
- email
- whatsapp
- referral
- partner

## 8. Monthly Reporting Metrics

Report monthly on:

- Website sessions
- Leads
- Qualified leads
- Cost per lead
- Cost per qualified lead
- Applications started
- Applications approved
- Funded deals
- Cost per funded deal
- Best-performing channel
- Worst-performing channel
- Wasted spend
- Lead response time
- Recommendations for next month

## 9. Offline Tracking

Some conversions may happen offline through phone, WhatsApp, or direct sales follow-up.

PropFlo must update lead statuses regularly so campaign performance can be understood properly.

Without lead status updates, reporting will only show enquiries, not real business value.

## 10. Tracking Limitations

Tracking may be affected by:

- Cookie consent
- Ad blockers
- Browser privacy settings
- WhatsApp conversations
- Phone calls
- Manual follow-up
- Incomplete CRM updates
- Cross-device behaviour

## 11. Pre-Launch Tracking Checklist

Before campaigns go live, confirm:

- Google Analytics 4 is installed
- Google Tag Manager is installed
- Form submissions are tracked
- Thank-you page is tracked
- Phone clicks are tracked
- WhatsApp clicks are tracked
- Google Ads conversions are linked
- UTM links are working
- Test leads appear in the lead tracker
- Lead source data is being captured
- Client receives lead notifications

## 12. Starting Recommendation

Do not launch paid ads until form submissions, phone clicks, WhatsApp clicks, and lead source tracking have been tested.
