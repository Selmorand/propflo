# PropFlo Monthly Tasks

## Purpose

This document defines the monthly management tasks for PropFlo marketing.

The goal is to create a repeatable month-end process for reporting, decision-making, optimisation, and planning.

## 1. Monthly Management Principle

The monthly review should answer one main question:

Is the marketing system producing qualified leads and funded deals at an acceptable cost?

## 2. Monthly Data To Collect

Collect the following data before preparing the report:

- Total ad spend
- Total website traffic
- Total leads
- Qualified leads
- Not-qualified leads
- Applications started
- Applications approved
- Deals funded
- Cost per lead
- Cost per qualified lead
- Cost per funded deal
- Best lead source
- Worst lead source
- Lead response time
- Main lost lead reasons
- Main not-qualified reasons

## 3. Google Ads Monthly Review

Review:

- Campaign performance
- Ad group performance
- Keyword performance
- Search terms
- Negative keywords added
- Cost per lead
- Cost per qualified lead
- Conversion rate
- Wasted spend
- Budget pacing
- Impression share, if useful
- Device performance
- Location performance

Actions:

- Pause weak keywords
- Expand strong keywords carefully
- Add new negative keywords
- Improve ad copy
- Adjust budgets
- Review landing page alignment
- Recommend next-month spend

## 4. Lead Quality Monthly Review

Review:

- Which channels produced qualified leads
- Which channels produced poor leads
- Which campaigns produced applications
- Which campaigns produced funded deals
- Which lead types were most valuable
- Which objections appeared repeatedly
- Which qualification rules caused drop-off

Actions:

- Improve form questions
- Improve landing page FAQs
- Adjust ad wording
- Improve sales handover process
- Recommend better targeting

## 5. Website and Landing Page Review

Review:

- Landing page visits
- Form submissions
- Form completion rate
- Phone clicks
- WhatsApp clicks
- FAQ engagement
- Page speed
- Mobile usability
- Common user questions

Actions:

- Add missing FAQs
- Improve CTA placement
- Clarify process wording
- Improve trust sections
- Remove confusing wording
- Add approved disclaimers where needed

## 6. SEO Monthly Review

Review:

- Organic traffic
- Google Search Console clicks
- Google Search Console impressions
- Top queries
- Top pages
- New content published
- FAQ performance
- Internal links
- Content gaps

Actions:

- Plan next articles
- Improve existing content
- Add internal links
- Add FAQs based on real enquiries
- Prioritise topics that support lead generation

## 7. Partner Marketing Monthly Review

Review:

- New partner contacts
- Partner replies
- Partner meetings
- Partner referrals
- Partner lead quality
- Estate agent response
- Conveyancer response
- Partner objections

Actions:

- Improve partner message
- Update partner FAQ
- Create better partner material
- Follow up with interested partners
- Identify best referral sources

## 8. Reporting Structure

The monthly report should include:

1. Executive summary
2. What happened this month
3. Performance by channel
4. Lead quality review
5. Sales pipeline review
6. Google Ads review
7. SEO and content review
8. Partner marketing review
9. Issues and limitations
10. Recommendations for next month
11. Budget recommendation
12. Action list

## 9. Monthly Strategy Questions

Ask:

1. Should budget increase, decrease, or stay the same?
2. Which channel deserves more attention?
3. Which channel should be reduced?
4. Are leads being handled fast enough?
5. Is PropFlo giving enough sales feedback?
6. Are campaigns attracting the right people?
7. Are qualification rules clear enough?
8. Are landing pages answering the right questions?
9. Are partner referrals worth expanding?
10. What is the biggest bottleneck?

## 10. Monthly Client Meeting

Recommended agenda:

1. Review previous month performance
2. Review lead quality
3. Review funded deal data
4. Review campaign changes
5. Review website/content improvements
6. Review sales feedback
7. Agree next-month priorities
8. Agree next-month budget
9. Confirm approvals needed

## 11. Monthly Deliverables

Deliver:

- Monthly report
- Optimisation summary
- Updated budget recommendation
- Updated action list
- Content plan for next month
- Campaign priorities for next month

## 12. Starting Recommendation

At the end of each month, do not only show marketing numbers.

Show what the numbers mean and what should happen next.
