# PropFlo Weekly Tasks

## Purpose

This document defines the weekly marketing management tasks for PropFlo.

The goal is to keep campaigns controlled, improve lead quality, and avoid wasting budget.

## 1. Weekly Management Principle

Weekly management should focus on useful business outcomes, not activity for the sake of activity.

The main question each week is:

Are the campaigns producing qualified leads that can become funded deals?

## 2. Google Ads Weekly Tasks

Review:

- Total spend
- Daily budget pacing
- Clicks
- Cost per click
- Conversions
- Cost per lead
- Search terms
- Poor-quality searches
- Negative keywords
- Keyword performance
- Ad copy performance
- Conversion tracking issues

Actions:

- Add negative keywords
- Pause poor search terms
- Reduce spend on weak ad groups
- Increase spend only where lead quality is strong
- Test one or two ad copy changes if needed
- Check that conversion tracking is still working

## 3. Lead Quality Review

Ask PropFlo for feedback on the week's leads.

Review:

- Total leads
- Qualified leads
- Not-qualified leads
- Duplicate leads
- Spam leads
- Applications started
- Documents requested
- Deals funded, if any
- Common reasons leads did not qualify

Actions:

- Adjust ad copy if leads misunderstand the offer
- Adjust landing page copy if expectations are unclear
- Adjust form questions if too many poor leads come through
- Adjust targeting if location or audience quality is poor

## 4. Landing Page Review

Check:

- Is the page loading correctly?
- Are forms working?
- Are phone and WhatsApp links working?
- Are disclaimers visible?
- Is the CTA clear?
- Are users dropping off before enquiry?
- Are repeated lead questions answered on the page?

Actions:

- Improve FAQ section
- Clarify qualification wording
- Improve CTA placement
- Add missing trust information
- Reduce unnecessary friction in the form

## 5. Tracking Review

Check:

- GA4 is receiving traffic
- Google Ads conversions are recording
- Form submissions are tracked
- Phone clicks are tracked
- WhatsApp clicks are tracked
- UTM links are working
- Lead source data is captured
- Test leads still work

Actions:

- Fix broken tracking quickly
- Note any tracking limitations in the optimisation log
- Confirm any offline leads are being updated manually

## 6. Partner Marketing Review

Check:

- New estate agent contacts
- New conveyancer contacts
- Partner replies
- Partner referrals
- Partner questions
- Follow-up tasks

Actions:

- Send partner follow-ups
- Record interested partners
- Improve partner message if needed
- Add partner FAQs based on real questions

## 7. Content Review

Check:

- Content published this week
- Content planned for next week
- FAQ updates needed
- Blog topics based on real enquiries
- LinkedIn or social posts scheduled
- Internal links added

Actions:

- Add new content ideas from sales questions
- Prioritise pages that support paid campaigns
- Avoid publishing unapproved finance claims

## 8. Weekly Meeting Notes

Each week, record:

- What changed
- What worked
- What did not work
- What was paused
- What was improved
- What feedback PropFlo gave
- What needs client approval
- What will be done next week

## 9. Weekly Warning Signs

Watch for:

- High spend with no qualified leads
- Many personal loan enquiries
- Slow lead response
- Tracking gaps
- Repeated confusion about qualification
- Ads disapproved by platform
- Leads outside target area
- High form abandonment
- No sales feedback from PropFlo

## 10. Starting Recommendation

During the first month after launch, review Google Ads and lead quality at least twice per week.

After the campaign stabilises, move to a formal weekly optimisation rhythm.
