# PropFlo Follow-Up Workflow

## Purpose

This document defines how PropFlo should respond to incoming leads.

The goal is to make sure no lead is ignored, delayed, duplicated, or lost without a clear reason.

## 1. Main Principle

Fast follow-up is critical.

A person enquiring about bridging finance may have an urgent cash-flow problem. If PropFlo responds slowly, the lead may lose trust or contact another provider.

## 2. Recommended Response Targets

### Ideal Response

Contact new leads within 15 minutes during working hours.

### Acceptable Response

Contact new leads within 1 hour during working hours.

### Same-Day Requirement

All leads received during working hours should be contacted the same day.

### After-Hours Leads

Leads received after hours should receive an automatic confirmation and be contacted the next working morning.

## 3. New Lead Workflow

When a new lead arrives:

1. Lead is captured from website, phone, WhatsApp, email, referral, or ad campaign.
2. Lead source is recorded.
3. Lead is marked as New Lead.
4. PropFlo receives notification.
5. PropFlo contacts the lead.
6. Lead is updated to Contact Attempted or Contacted.
7. Qualification questions are asked.
8. Lead is moved to Qualified or Not Qualified.
9. If qualified, documents are requested.
10. Lead is followed until funded, declined, lost, or closed.

## 4. First Contact Script

The first contact should be simple and calm.

Suggested wording:

Good day, this is [Name] from PropFlo. Thank you for your enquiry. I would like to confirm a few details about your property sale so we can see whether PropFlo may be able to assist.

Before using this script, PropFlo must approve the wording.

## 5. Basic Qualification Questions

Ask:

1. Has the property been sold?
2. Has an Offer to Purchase been signed?
3. Has a transferring attorney been appointed?
4. Where is the property located?
5. What is the estimated sale value?
6. How much funding is needed?
7. When is transfer expected?
8. What is the reason for needing the funds?
9. What is the best way to contact you?

## 6. If the Lead Qualifies

If the lead appears to qualify:

1. Mark as Qualified.
2. Explain the next step.
3. Request required documents.
4. Confirm how documents should be submitted.
5. Set next follow-up date.
6. Update the CRM or lead tracker.

## 7. If the Lead Does Not Qualify

If the lead does not qualify:

1. Mark as Not Qualified.
2. Record the reason.
3. Respond politely.
4. Do not promise future approval.
5. Do not provide financial advice unless PropFlo has approved the process.

Suggested wording:

Based on the information provided, it does not look like this application fits the current PropFlo requirements. Thank you for your enquiry.

Final wording must be approved by PropFlo.

## 8. If the Lead Does Not Answer

If the lead does not answer:

1. Mark as Contact Attempted.
2. Send a short WhatsApp or email if consent allows.
3. Try again later.
4. Make a second attempt the next working day.
5. Close as Lost only after the agreed number of attempts.

Suggested attempt schedule:

- Attempt 1: Within 15 minutes
- Attempt 2: Same day
- Attempt 3: Next working day
- Attempt 4: Two working days later

## 9. Document Follow-Up

If documents are requested:

1. Mark as Documents Requested.
2. Send document checklist.
3. Confirm submission method.
4. Follow up if not received.
5. Mark as Documents Received once complete.

## 10. Application Follow-Up

Once documents are received:

1. Mark as Application Submitted.
2. Confirm review process.
3. Update the lead when approved or declined.
4. If approved, mark as Approved.
5. If offer is sent, mark as Offer Sent.
6. If accepted, mark as Offer Accepted.
7. If funded, mark as Funded.

## 11. Lost Lead Reasons

Record why a lead was lost.

Common reasons:

- Could not contact
- Not ready
- Used another provider
- Transfer completed
- Did not send documents
- Did not accept terms
- Not comfortable with process
- Fees too high
- Approval took too long
- No longer needed funds

## 12. Daily Lead Review

PropFlo should review open leads daily.

Check:

- New leads not contacted
- Contact attempted leads
- Qualified leads waiting for documents
- Documents requested but not received
- Applications waiting for review
- Offers sent but not accepted

## 13. Weekly Lead Review

Interon and PropFlo should review lead quality weekly during the launch phase.

Review:

- Which source produced the best leads?
- Which source produced poor leads?
- Why were leads not qualified?
- Were leads contacted fast enough?
- Are forms asking the right questions?
- Are ads attracting the wrong people?
- Are landing pages creating confusion?

## 14. Starting Recommendation

Before campaigns go live, PropFlo must confirm who receives leads and how quickly they will respond.

A good campaign can fail if leads are not handled quickly and consistently.
