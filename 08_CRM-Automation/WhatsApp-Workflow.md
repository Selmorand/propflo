# PropFlo WhatsApp Workflow

## Purpose

This document defines how WhatsApp should be used for PropFlo lead follow-up.

The goal is to make communication fast, useful, professional, and controlled.

## 1. Main Principle

WhatsApp should support the sales process.

It should not be used casually for sensitive finance discussions unless PropFlo confirms that the process is acceptable.

Keep messages short, clear, and approved.

## 2. Recommended WhatsApp Use

WhatsApp may be used for:

- Confirming that an enquiry was received
- Asking when the client is available for a call
- Sending a basic next-step message
- Sending a document checklist
- Following up on missing documents
- Confirming that a consultant will contact the lead
- Communicating with referral partners

## 3. What WhatsApp Should Not Be Used For Without Approval

Do not use WhatsApp for the following unless PropFlo has approved the process:

- Collecting sensitive financial information
- Requesting confidential documents
- Sending full finance offers
- Discussing detailed repayment terms
- Making approval promises
- Confirming guaranteed funding
- Sharing private client information with third parties

## 4. New Lead WhatsApp Message

### Timing

After a website enquiry is received, if WhatsApp contact is allowed.

### Suggested Message

Good day [First Name], this is [Name] from PropFlo.

Thank you for your enquiry. We have received your details and would like to confirm a few points about your property sale so we can see whether PropFlo may be able to assist.

When would be a convenient time to call you?

## 5. No Answer Follow-Up

### Timing

If the lead did not answer the phone.

### Suggested Message

Good day [First Name], this is [Name] from PropFlo.

We tried to reach you regarding your enquiry. Please let us know when it would be convenient to call you back.

## 6. Document Checklist Message

### Timing

After the lead appears to qualify and PropFlo requests documents.

### Suggested Message

Good day [First Name].

To continue with your PropFlo enquiry, please prepare the documents requested by your consultant.

These may include your signed Offer to Purchase, property details, transferring attorney details, and identity document.

Please only send documents through the approved PropFlo process.

## 7. Document Reminder Message

### Timing

One working day after documents were requested, if not received.

### Suggested Message

Good day [First Name].

This is a reminder that we are waiting for the requested documents for your PropFlo enquiry.

Once received, the team can continue reviewing your application.

## 8. Not Qualified Message

### Timing

After PropFlo confirms the lead does not qualify.

### Suggested Message

Good day [First Name].

Based on the information provided, it does not look like this enquiry fits the current PropFlo requirements.

Thank you for contacting us.

## 9. Final Follow-Up Message

### Timing

After agreed follow-up attempts fail.

### Suggested Message

Good day [First Name].

We have not been able to continue with your PropFlo enquiry.

If you still need assistance, please reply to this message. Otherwise, your enquiry may be marked as inactive.

## 10. Partner WhatsApp Message

### Purpose

For estate agents, conveyancers, or referral partners.

### Suggested Message

Good day [First Name], this is [Name] from PropFlo.

We help qualifying property sellers who may need access to funds linked to their property sale before transfer is complete.

If you work with sellers who may need this type of assistance, we can explain the referral process and approved wording.

Would you like us to send you the partner information?

## 11. Opt-Out Message

If a person asks not to be contacted again, record the opt-out immediately.

Suggested response:

No problem. We have noted your request and will not contact you further regarding this enquiry.

## 12. Tone Rules

Messages should be:

- Short
- Calm
- Professional
- Clear
- Non-pushy
- Free of exaggerated claims
- Free of pressure tactics

Avoid phrases like:

- Guaranteed approval
- Instant cash
- No questions asked
- We can definitely help
- You are approved
- Urgent offer
- Limited-time finance

## 13. Internal Process

Every WhatsApp interaction should be recorded in the lead tracker or CRM.

Record:

- Date
- Message sent
- Lead response
- Next follow-up date
- Lead stage
- Notes

## 14. Compliance Notes

Before these messages are used, PropFlo must approve:

- Product wording
- Qualification wording
- Document request wording
- Disclaimer wording
- Privacy wording
- Opt-out process

## 15. Starting Recommendation

Start with only three WhatsApp messages:

1. New lead message
2. No answer follow-up
3. Document checklist

Add more messages only after the real sales process is tested.
