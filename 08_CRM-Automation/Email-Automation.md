# PropFlo Email Automation

## Purpose

This document defines the recommended email follow-up structure for PropFlo leads.

The goal is to help leads understand the next step, reduce confusion, and improve conversion from enquiry to application.

## 1. Main Principle

Email automation should support the sales process.

It should not replace human follow-up.

For bridging finance enquiries, people may need quick human contact, so email should confirm, guide, and reassure.

## 2. Email Sequence Overview

Recommended basic sequence:

1. Enquiry received confirmation
2. Document checklist
3. Follow-up reminder
4. FAQ and next steps
5. Final follow-up before closing as inactive

## 3. Email 1: Enquiry Received

### Timing

Immediately after form submission.

### Purpose

Confirm that the enquiry was received.

### Suggested Subject

Your PropFlo enquiry has been received

### Suggested Body

Good day [First Name],

Thank you for your enquiry.

Your information has been received and a PropFlo consultant will review your details.

If your enquiry was sent during working hours, someone will contact you as soon as possible. If it was sent after hours, you should be contacted on the next working day.

Please note that any assistance is subject to qualification, verification, approval, and PropFlo's terms and conditions.

Kind regards,  
The PropFlo Team

## 4. Email 2: Document Checklist

### Timing

After the lead is marked as qualified or when documents are requested.

### Purpose

Explain what the client should prepare.

### Suggested Subject

Documents needed for your PropFlo application

### Suggested Body

Good day [First Name],

To continue with your PropFlo enquiry, please prepare the documents requested by your consultant.

These may include:

- Signed Offer to Purchase
- Seller identity document
- Property details
- Transferring attorney details
- Bond or settlement information, if applicable
- Any additional documents requested by PropFlo

Please only send documents through the approved PropFlo process.

Kind regards,  
The PropFlo Team

## 5. Email 3: Follow-Up Reminder

### Timing

One working day after documents are requested if not received.

### Purpose

Remind the client without pressure.

### Suggested Subject

Reminder: PropFlo application documents

### Suggested Body

Good day [First Name],

This is a reminder that we are waiting for the documents requested for your PropFlo enquiry.

Once the documents are received, the PropFlo team can continue reviewing your application.

If you no longer need assistance, please let us know.

Kind regards,  
The PropFlo Team

## 6. Email 4: FAQ and Next Steps

### Timing

Two to three working days after enquiry if the lead has not progressed.

### Purpose

Answer common questions and reduce uncertainty.

### Suggested Subject

PropFlo next steps and common questions

### Suggested Body

Good day [First Name],

We understand that property transfer and bridging finance can raise many questions.

Common questions include:

- Who qualifies?
- What documents are needed?
- How long does approval take?
- What happens if transfer is delayed?
- How is repayment handled?

A PropFlo consultant can guide you through the next step based on your situation.

Please note that all applications are subject to qualification, verification, approval, and PropFlo's terms and conditions.

Kind regards,  
The PropFlo Team

## 7. Email 5: Final Follow-Up

### Timing

Five to seven working days after no response.

### Purpose

Close the loop politely.

### Suggested Subject

Should we keep your PropFlo enquiry open?

### Suggested Body

Good day [First Name],

We have not been able to continue with your PropFlo enquiry.

If you still need assistance, please reply to this email or contact PropFlo directly.

If we do not hear from you, your enquiry may be marked as inactive.

Kind regards,  
The PropFlo Team

## 8. Partner Email Sequence

For estate agents, conveyancers, and other partners, use a separate sequence.

Suggested partner emails:

1. Thank you for your partner enquiry
2. How the referral process works
3. Approved wording for discussing PropFlo with sellers
4. Partner FAQ
5. Follow-up from the PropFlo team

## 9. Important Compliance Notes

Before these emails are used, PropFlo must approve:

- Product wording
- Qualification wording
- Disclaimer wording
- Document request wording
- Terms and conditions wording
- Privacy and consent wording

## 10. Starting Recommendation

Start with only the first two emails:

1. Enquiry received
2. Document checklist

Do not over-automate until the real sales process is tested.
